    What to Consider When Buying Moisturizers for Acne-prone Skin?

    Before you invest in a moisturizer for your acne-prone skin, take a look at the points to keep in mind-

    a) Formula: Formula plays the biggest role when it comes to choosing the best moisturizer for your skin. Always stick to a lightweight formula that doesn’t clog pores. A non-comedogenic product doesn’t clog your pores, thus, preventing further acne.

    b) Ingredients: Natural ingredients like tea tree oil, aloe vera, and turmeric, are good for acne-prone skin. Other than these, vitamin B3, hyaluronic acid, and salicylic acid work wonder on acne-prone skin. Avoid petroleum and alcohol-based products to further worsen your skin problems.

    c) Texture: The texture depends on your skin type! If you have oily acne-prone skin, better to stick to the non-greasy, mattifying gel. On the other hand, if you have dry acne-prone skin, choose a product with a lot of hydrating components.

    d) Skin condition: People with certain skin conditions like eczema, rosacea, cystic acne, and redness, may need to pick their product carefully. In their case, an alcohol-based product is a big no-no as it will further worsen the condition.

    FAQ's

    Can moisturizers really control pimples?

    Your skin produces more sebum and oil to keep itself nourished! Acne is mostly caused by clogged pores by the accumulation of sebum and oil. When you don’t keep the skin moisturized enough, your skin produces more sebum to keep itself moisturized naturally, and thus, you witness more breakouts. Choosing effective moisturizers typically made for your acne-prone skin controls pimples and gives radiant skin.

    Is moisturizer good for acne scars?

    Moisturizers that come with active ingredients and gentle acids targeting blemishes work wonders for your skin.

    Can I use moisturizer for oily skin regularly?

    Of course. You shouldn’t skip your moisturizer at any cost to keep every skin-related problem at bay.

    How often should you apply moisturizers for acne-prone skin?

    You should apply a moisturizer twice a day after following a cleansing and toning ritual to get the best result out of it.
