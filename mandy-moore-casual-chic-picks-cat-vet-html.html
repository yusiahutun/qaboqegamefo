Mandy Moore looks casual and chic in jeans and a white T-shirt as she picks up her cat from the vet

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Mandy Moore looked casual in Glendale, California where she picked up her cat from the vet on Wednesday.

The actress, 39, originally from New Hampshire, looked effortlessly chic in a short sleeved white T-shirt.

Mandy, whose hair was styled in loose shoulder-length waves, also wore light blue cut-off jeans and a pair of bright red sandals.

However, the This is Us star's biggest accessory was the pet carrier that she cradled in both hands. 

She covered the crate with a white scarf to protect her cat from the strong Californian sun.  

Casual: Mandy Moore picked up her cat from the vets in Glendale, California on Wednesday wearing light blue jeans and a short-sleeved white T-shirt

Colourful: Mandy finished off her casual look with a pair of bright red sandals as she passes a dog outside the vets

This comes after Mandy posted a photo on Instagram of her getting her back realigned, a treatment for her scoliosis. 

'Mid back adjustment for lumbar and pelvic alignment.' She wrote. 'I look forward to this all week!' 

The Candy hitmaker, who turns 40 next year, recently expressed her appreciation for her ex Andy Roddick, a tennis ace. 

Mandy dated Roddick between 2003 and 2004. She is now happily married to Taylor Goldsmith, the lead singer of folk rock band Dawes.

She owns three rescue cats and describes herself as a 'life-long cat lover'.
