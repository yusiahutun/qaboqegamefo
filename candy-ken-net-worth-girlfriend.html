How much money does Candy Ken make? His Net Worth as of 2022

Candy Ken is an Austrian rapper, model, TikToker as well as and artist. He is best known for his juxtaposition of masculinity with feminine pop culture influences.

Early Life of Candy Ken

Candy Ken entered this world on 27th July 1992, in Bregenz, Austria. His birth name is Jakob Kasimir Hellrigl while his horoscope is Leo.

Candy holds an Austrian nationality while his ethnic background is Caucasian. Ever since Candy was a kid, he was passionate and interested in rapping, fashion, and modeling.

Education

He studied photography and film design at the Lette-Verein College in Berlin but dropped out of his studies in the year 2014. Later, he joined Wenatchee High School in Wenatchee, Washington for a year where he played football.

Professional Career

  • While he was studying at college, Candy created the gender-bending persona as Candy Ken which is incorporating imagery from Hello Kitty, Japanese Kawaii culture, Barbie dolls as well as pink clothing in his dress style and visual appearance.
  • His self-directed video series was released on YouTube in the year 2015.
  • In September 2015, Candy released the second EP Candy Ken Paradise produced by DJ Smokera and distributed by Spinnup.
  • He released a third EP, Daddy 69, containing five songs and five videos in November 2015.
  • Candy directed four videos and the video for the song 69 is directed by London-based underground director Kassandra Powell.
  • In April 2018, he appeared on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on a segment of Guess Who’s High. Candy released his first album Real Talk containing twelve songs in June 2016.
  • The release of his album was promoted with a club party at Visions Video Bar in London.
  • The ten songs of his album were made in collaboration with the rapper Gigi Tays.
  • Further, it was also produced by Smokera and the video for the song, What I Like directed by Powell.
  • Candy mentions the American rapper Riff Raff as his main musical and artistic influence for the album.

Modeling Career

  • Candy started his modeling career when he was noticed on Instagram in the year 2015 by stylist and Diesel brand creative designer Nicola Formichetti.
  • At that time, Nicola invited Candy to do a photo shoot for a photo session in Formichetti’s Japanese magazine FREE.
  • Later, Nicola introduced him to the photographer Terry Richardson, who also made a photoshoot with him in full frontal nudity for the magazine Man About Town.
  • At the same time, Candy was booked as a fashion model by the Italian Diesel clothing brand at Pride Week in New York City.
  • Also, he made a runway appearance at the opening for designer Roberto Piqueras’ fashion show at Berlin’s alternative fashion week, and for Japanese fashion designer Michiko Koshino.
  • Candy has been in photoshoot sessions for VMAN magazine, Paper magazine, Vogue Taiwan, and the Korean edition of Dazed magazine.
