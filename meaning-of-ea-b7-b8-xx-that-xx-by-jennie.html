Meaning of XX (THAT XX) by JENNIE

Publish date: 2024-02-28

The JENNIE song titled '그 XX (THAT XX)' is a powerful and emotional track that delves into the complexities of love, heartbreak, and the struggles of unrequited feelings. The overall theme of the song revolves around the narrator's frustration and sadness over a love interest who is involved with someone else, highlighting the pain of loving someone who cannot reciprocate those feelings. The lyrics express a range of emotions, from anger and jealousy to longing and resignation, making it a relatable and poignant song for many listeners.One standout lyric in the song is "Oh, 날 몰라주는 네가 미워" (Oh, I hate you for not recognizing me). This lyric showcases the speaker's frustration and resentment towards the person they love, who seems to be oblivious to their feelings. This theme of unrequited love is further explored in the line "그 새끼보다 내가 못한 게 뭐야?" (What have I done that's worse than that jerk?). Here, the narrator questions their self-worth and wonders why they are not chosen over the person their love interest is with. This lyric emphasizes feelings of inadequacy, as the narrator struggles to understand why they are not worthy of love.Another significant lyric that contributes to the overall theme of the song is "널 위해 거짓말할게" (I'll lie for you). This lyric suggests that the narrator is willing to deceive others and compromise their own feelings for the sake of protecting the person they love. It portrays the sacrifices one is willing to make in the hopes of being closer to their love interest, even if it means hiding their pain and putting on a mask of happiness. This theme of selflessness in love adds depth to the song, illustrating the lengths the narrator is willing to go to support their love interest.The line "그래 널 위해 거짓말할게" (I'm sorry, I'll lie for you) further emphasizes the theme of sacrifice in love. It reveals the narrator's willingness to take responsibility for their love interest's unhappiness, even if it means shouldering the blame for any pain they may cause. This theme of self-blame and the desire to protect the person they love at all costs adds an element of emotional complexity to the song, as the narrator grapples with their own heartache while attempting to shield their love interest.Additionally, the lyric "Yeah, 비싼 차, 예쁜 옷, 고급 레스토랑, 넌 잘 어울려" (Yeah, expensive cars, pretty clothes, fancy restaurants, you fit in well) introduces the theme of materialism and societal expectations. This lyric highlights the narrator's observation that their love interest seems to be more compatible with someone who possesses material wealth and status. It reflects the struggle of feeling inadequate compared to those who can offer more lavish lifestyles, further intensifying the narrator's feelings of insecurity and unworthiness.In summary, the JENNIE song '그 XX (THAT XX)' delves into the complexities of unrequited love, heartbreak, sacrifice, and the societal pressures that can affect relationships. The standout lyrics discussed above help to develop these themes, painting a vivid picture of the narrator's emotional journey. Through its emotional depth and relatable themes, this song resonates with listeners who have experienced the pain and confusion that comes with loving someone who cannot return those feelings.
