Hailey Bieber's new tattoo convinces fans it's a 'sign' - but blink and you'll miss it

Publish date: 2024-07-05

The model and skincare brand founder has revealed she has a new tattoo, but Hailey Bieber fans are convinced it’s a sign she’s pregnant. This isn’t the first time the star has had to deny pregnancy rumors, as followers seem to look for ‘signs’ regularly.

This isn’t her first tattoo the celebrity has had. Hailey has more than 20 already, with at least one dedicated to her husband of five years, Justin Bieber. Now she has shared her new addition, which she called a “tiny baby tat” on Instagram. The inking has some fans talking as they question her choice of caption.

Hailey Bieber’s new tattoo

Taking to her Stories yesterday (December 20), Hailey shared a picture of the side of her hand where the new tattoo now lies. The ink is a small bow joins quite a few other tattoos on her hand, such as some simple dots on her fingers.

Hailey didn’t give followers an insight into what it meant, but bow tattoos are often associated with femininity, grace, and even loyalty.

Talking about her tattoos, Hailey explained the first one she received was the date her parents.

She explained: “That was the first tattoo I ever got, and I would say probably in terms of being meaningful, that would be the most meaningful one that I have.”

One getting more, she said she was saving space to add to her body art collection.

The model added:”There are so many more [tattoos] I know I want to do in life. Right now, I’m saving space to get something that has to do with my kids in the future.”

Fans react to the tattoo

On X (formerly Twitter) fans have been questioning the caption and her use of the words “tiny” and “baby.” To some followers, they’re convinced it’s connected to having a child, but the star has spoken out before over pregnancy rumors.

What has Hailey said about having children?

The Hailey Bieber tattoo speculation will leave fans wondering if and when the celebrity couple will start a family.

Hailey has said she and her music star husband do want children. She admitted she wants them “so bad” and cries over it “all the time.”

“I want kids so bad but I get scared,” she said. “It’s enough that people say things about my husband or my friends. I can’t imagine having to confront people saying things about a child.

“We can only do the best we can to raise them,” she continued. “As long as they feel loved and safe.”
