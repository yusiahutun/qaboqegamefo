Hottest and Coldest Temperatures Ever in Philadelphia on November 11

Publish date: 2024-09-24

On November 11th in Philadelphia, the weather typically experiences an average temperature of 47.27°F, with an average high of 53.34°F and an average low of 41.19°F. However, on this date in 2006, the city experienced a record-breaking heatwave with temperatures reaching a scorching 73.9°F. In contrast, back in 1961, Philadelphia endured a bone-chilling cold spell, as temperatures dropped to a frigid 21.0°F, setting the record for the coldest November 11th to date. As a fun tidbit, in 2006, Philadelphia hosted the World Series Parade, celebrating the Phillies' victory after a 28-year championship drought. The city came alive as fans filled the streets, braving the unseasonably warm weather to show their support and jubilation.
