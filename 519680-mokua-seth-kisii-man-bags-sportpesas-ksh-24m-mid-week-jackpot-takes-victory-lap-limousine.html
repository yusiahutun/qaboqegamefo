Mokua Seth: Kisii Man Bags Sportpesa's KSh 24m Mid-Week Jackpot, Takes Victory Lap in Limousine

Publish date: 2024-02-16
  • The SportPesa mid-week jackpot played between August 30 and 31 had a winner going home with upward of KSh 24.6 million
  • Seth Mokua predicted 13 matches correctly, winning the whooping amount that several other bettors were running after
  • Before getting to the betting firm's office in Nairobi, Mokua was given a kingly treatment that included being chauffeured in a limousine sandwiched in a sirened motorcade

Nairobi - Seth Mokua, a native of Kisii county, was unveiled as the winner of SportPesa's mid-week jackpot.

KSh 24.6 million was up for grabs for correct predictions of the 13 football matches played.

The bonanza was played between August 30 and 31.

SportPesa would then announce on Friday, September 1, that a winner had been found.

"We have a winner!! SportPesa is proud to announce KSh 24,665,485 Midweek Jackpot has been won. Stay tuned, more details are coming," they said.

"Meet Mokua Seth, KSh 24,665,485 SportPesa Midweek Jackpot winner!"

He was given a kingly treatment which included being chauffeured in a limousine sandwiched in a motorcade akin to a presidential escort.
