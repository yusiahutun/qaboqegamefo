Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively looking for 'multi-million home' 3000 miles away

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds are reportedly looking to “put down roots” in the UK with a “multi-million” home in London, 3000 miles away from their home in Pound Ridge, New York.

The Hollywood power couple spends more time in the UK after the Deadpool star purchased the football club Wrexham AFC with fellow actor Rob McElhenney in 2020. New reports state that Blake and Ryan Reynolds are looking for another “home” in the UK after they purchased a property in Wales.

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds to “put down roots” in the UK

Opposite Adele’s move to California to pursue her American dream, Blake Lively and Ryan are reportedly looking to “put down roots” in the UK thanks to their mutual business interests across the pond.

The Deadpool star has expressed his love for the UK in several interviews and it looks like the whole family enjoys spending more time outside of their home country.

As reported by Daily Mail, a source close to the pair told the outlet: “Ryan and Blake just love Britain, it has become a real second home for them. They have made some really good friends in the UK, so it made sense to have a proper base here.”

The source continued: “While they love Wrexham, a lot of their work is taking place in London so it seemed to make more sense to be there rather than Wales. The UK is small compared to America, so nowhere is actually that far.”

The couple bought Welsh property

The new reports come after Blake and Ryan reportedly purchased a £1.5million house in Marford, a village in North Wales. The celebrity star is a football club owner and wants to stay as close as possible to run his business.

The Deadpool star purchased the Welsh property after he and fellow actor Rob McElhenney took over Wrexham AFC in 2020.

The North Wales village is home to 2,500 residents and the property acts as another home to Ryan, Blake, and their four kids when they visit the UK.

Blake and Ryan have welcomed four children together, James, 8, Inez, 6, Betty, 3, and a baby, whose name or sex the two actors haven’t yet revealed.

Blake brought two of their kids to Deadpool 3 set

Ryan and Blake were recently spotted on the set of Deadpool 3 after the Gossip Girl TV star surprised her husband when she brought two of their children.

Ryan and his co-star Hugh Jackman, who plays Wolverine in the upcoming film, were filming scenes outside of London before the SAG-AFTRA strike began.

Several of the viral snaps showed Ryan and his children having adorable moments together, while another snap showed one of the actor’s daughters carrying a figurine of Wolverine.
