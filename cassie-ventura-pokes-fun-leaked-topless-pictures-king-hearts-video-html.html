Topless pictures of the sassy star were leaked online a few years ago so fans of Cassie Ventura won’t be all that shocked to see her flash the flesh once again.

In a move which the 25-year-old labelled ‘foul and evil’, at home shots of her did the rounds on gossip websites back in 2009, providing a much-needed boost to her flagging music career.

This time the singer has chosen to flaunt her ample chest on her terms in the new video for her upcoming track King of Hearts.

The starlet can be seen parading around a beautiful Malibu mansion in a barely there top

The starlet can be seen parading around a beautiful Malibu mansion in a barely there top, which is reminiscent of glamour model Jodie Marsh’s infamous three-belt outfit.

Donning high-waisted black briefs, which only just conceal her bikini line, Cassie puts Lady GaGa to shame as she shows her take on the dominatrix look, which seems to have taken the music world by storm lately.

As a mysterious man carrying a camera lens is seen arriving in a vehicle outside the location, shots cut between Ventura wriggling around on a coach ready to have her picture taken.

Looking good: The 25-year-old singer had nude pictures of herself leaked online two years ago but is now baring her toned physique on her own terms

Climbing the ladder: Cassie is a protege of rapper Sean 'Diddy' Combs and his rumoured girlfriend

The pace quickens before Cassie is seen playing with a lighter flame which she then drops onto a vinyl record.

Poking fun at her controversial past, the clips are perhaps symbolic of P.Diddy’s protégée leaving one chapter of her career behind.

Speaking on BET’s 106 & Park, she said: ‘I shot it with Chris Sims and he has done a lot of rock videos in the past so I wanted to try something different, something just different from me.

‘It was great, a lot of fun.’

Getting hot: Cassie takes a soak in a bath by the fireplace

Meanwhile the star – who has worn her hairstyle with almost half a shaved head for many years – received great praise from her proud mentor, Diddy.

He added: ‘To me, she’s definitely one of the baddest in the game. Trust me. On this album, she’s gonna show y’all what she got just like she did on the last album.’
