David Beckham reveals new tattoo of Jay Z's lyrics

Publish date: 2024-08-28

    David Beckham has added another tattoo to his extensive collection.

    The former England footballer now has some of rapper Jay Z's lyrics, "Dream Big, Be Unrealistic", inked on his right hand.

    David showcased the tattoo on Wednesday evening at a book signing event at luxury menswear store Belstaff in New York.

    The mantra David has chosen is clearly significant for both himself and his wife Victoria Beckham, who tweeted the phrase after seeing Jay Z and Beyoncé in concert.

    "Dream big, be unrealistic," wrote the fashion designer back in August. "Jay Z at the Rose Bowl last night, Beyoncé and Jay Z #ontheruntour, AMAZING!!!! Xvb."

    David's latest inking comes three months after he unveiled new body art above the knuckle on his left hand.

    At the launch of his documentary, for which he journeyed into the heart of the Amazon rainforest, David revealed the words "Lead With Love", tattooed in calligraphy on his hand.
