Snooki has something to get off her chest: she got a boob job!

According to Nicole, she got C implants and a lift, opting against going any bigger.

Polizzi surprises Jenni with the big reveal around the 2:45 mark, after Farley fails to notice her enhanced chest.

"I got my t--s done," she finally tells her.

"Wait a minute, you actually did," Jwoww reacts. "I thought you were wearing a really good sports bra! Your boobs are fantastic."

Snooki says she's already expecting some backlash from the mommy shamers out there, but explains that now's as good a time as any for implants. "Our bodies are f--ked from having kids," she exclaims.

toofab's Brian Particelli spoke with Nicole about mommy shaming just last month, when she visited our studio for an interview.

"Mom shaming is really bad and it's one thing that me and Jenni hate," she said at the time. "Moms are supposed to support each other and build each other up and relate to each other. Everyone's going to have different parenting advice and how they do things."
