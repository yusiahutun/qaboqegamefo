Nyandarua National Polytechnic fee structure for 2023-24

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Have you completed your secondary education and searching for a Technical Training Institute to enroll in? Nyandarua National Polytechnic should be at the top of your list. This is an accredited public TVET institution offering various technical, engineering, hospitality, and business courses. But, before you enroll for any course, you should check out the Nyandarua National Polytechnic fee structure.

Nyandarua National Polytechnic is located along the Gilgil-Nyahururu Road in Nyahururu Town. The first batch of students were admitted to NYNP in 2006. Currently, the student population stands at more than 3500. Its vision is to become a leading global centre of excellence in technical and vocational training, research, innovation, and technology.
