Where the cast of 90 Day fianc season 1 are now including couples still together Tue 5 Decemb

Publish date: 2024-05-31

With 90 Day Fiance nearing the end of season 10, it means the cast of season 1 are celebrating one decade since their whirlwind romances.

90 Day Fiance has documented international relationships since its 2014 debut and as the franchise grows, a happily ever after seems increasingly unlikely for the TLC couples. Season 1 featured only four couples and we’re delighted to announce that some of them are stronger than ever as they mark their 10-year anniversaries in 2023.

Where 90 Day Fiance season 1 cast are in 2023

Russ and Paola

Owasso, Oklahoma resident Russ Mayfield fell in love with Colombia native Paola and quickly became one of the franchise’s favorite couples. Upon meeting in the US, issues quickly became apparent between the pair. Paola was uncomfortable living with her future in-laws and felt the pressure of being in a single-income household as Russ had lost his job. Paola, 27, built a modeling career online but Russ wasn’t delighted with her job given his conservative upbringing.

The pair married and welcomed their son, Axel, on January 1, 2019. The Mayfields took a break in August 2021 after eight years of marriage but have since reconciled. They celebrated their 10-year anniversary in October.

Alan and Kirlyam

Alan Cox met Kirlyam during a missionary trip in her hometown of Goiânia, Brazil. Culture shocks were expected as Cox was raised with Mormon values and found difficulty accepting Kirlyam’s modeling ambitions.

Nevertheless, they tied the knot in a Mormon temple in September 2013 while Kirlyam’s family, unable to attend, watched via livestream. 

The couple welcomed their sons, Liam and Enzo, in October 2017 and May 2021, respectively. They are still together in 2023.

View Instagram Post

Louis and Aya

The couple first met on an online dating service and despite Aya hailing from the Philippines, Louis and Aya proved distance couldn’t stop their relationship. Indianapolis resident Louis almost became the first US star to move abroad on 90 Day, but Aya eventually relocated to Louis since he had two young children.

The family expanded when Aya gave birth to their sons, Giovanni and Stefano in June 2018 and November 2020, respectively. The couple are still married in 2023.

View Instagram Post

Mike and Aziza

Mike and Aziza faced intimacy and affection issues at the start but they clearly worked through them as they tied the knot in October 2013. Their daughter, Olivia John, was born on January 14, 2019.

The Eloshways have not commented on their relationship status so we can presume they are still married.
