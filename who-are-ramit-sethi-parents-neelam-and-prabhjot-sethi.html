Neelam And Prabhjot Sethi! Age, Job

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Financial advisor and author Ramit Sethi after having taught his readers to become rich has now also come on television. An unscripted series for Netflix based on his 2009 New York Times Bestseller I Will Teach You to Be Rich dropped on National Tax Day on 18 April 2023.

Ramit, who grew up in a middle-class family in California, has been a contributor at Entrepreneur, among other things. In 2019, he issued a second edition of his book, which includes a 6-week program on how people can manage their money more effectively. Between all this, the self-made millionaire also has been hosting his own podcast under the same title “I Will Teach You to Be Rich”. In this writing though, we shall instead tell you about his parents, Neelam And Prabhjot Sethi.

Who Are Ramit Sethi’s Parents?

Ramit Sethi is clearly very fond of his parents.

Only recently, he took to his IG @ramit, to reveal proudly that he was able to bring his parents while filming his Netflix show. Both the folks had traveled hours from their home with a home-cooked Indian meal for the entire team. He gushed that over 20 of them were able to enjoy their food. “It was a hit”, in Ramit’s words, also the highlight of his day. In fact, today he also remembers that day as “definitely” a highlight of filming the show, all together.

Ramit has said this time and again — that a part of his Rich Life is having friends and family along the journey with him. Also, the way Ramit teased “Will our parents be in the show?! Check it out on April 18th”, his parents may be likely also appear on the show.

Meet Neelam Sethi, Ramit Sethi Mother

Here’s what we know about Ramit Sethi’s mother Neelam Sethi.

  • Neelam Sethi Age

Because Neelam S Sethi was born in June 1950, she turned 72 in 2022.

  • Neelam Sethi Job

Neelam Sethi long ago retired from her last job as a teacher at the Earl Le Gette Elementary School In Fair Oaks, California.

Long ago, she also studied at the Regional College of Education and Nirmala Convent in Mysore.

  • Is Neelam Sethi On Facebook?

Yes. Neelam Sethi could be given a follow on ‘Neelam Sethi’ Facebook as of 19 April 2023.

Meet Prabhjot Sethi, Ramit Sethi’s Father

Prabhjot Sethi and his wife Neelam have instilled this family tradition of taking one’s spouse to the Taj Mahal on their honeymoon. According to Ramit, he also saw his uncle and aunt do that.

So, Ramit too took his wife Cassandra Sethi to the Taj Mahal on their honeymoon. He said he started saving for his wedding and honeymoon in his early twenties, well before he met Cass.

When we were finally able to sit down and plan it, it was a feeling of freedom. The question wasn’t about how much it would cost. It was, “What do we want to do? We can do anything.”

We ended up going to multiple continents over 6 weeks and brought our loved ones with us for some parts.

Here, we had just taken an amazing trip through India. It was just us two at dawn. There was almost nobody here. It was this beautiful memory of us just being together.

  • Prabhjot Sethi Age

Prabhjot S Sethi was born in November 1943. So, he turned 79 years old in 2022.

  • Prabhjot Sethi Job

On his Facebook, Prabhjot Sethi mentioned working at California Energy Commission College. Also, he briefly informed us about studying at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business and the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

  • Prabhjot Sethi On Facebook?

Prabhjot Sethi also could be found on Facebook under his own name at the this of this writing.

Related FAQs

  • Are Ramit Sethi’s Parents Still Married?

Indeed. Ramit Sethi’s parents are still together and married.

On 8 January 2021, the folks celebrated their 45th marriage anniversary. On the occasion, their son Maneesh took to his social media to call them an incredible pair of people who raised an awesome family. This time around, Prabhjot was also living his early retirement days. “45 years! Imagine that sort of time together. From an arranged marriage no less”, Maneesh also wrote before ensuring that he is proud to be their son.

  • Where Do Ramit Sethi’s Parents Reside?

Ramit Sethi’s mom on her Facebook mentioned Fair Oaks as her hometown and also her current place of residence.

Her husband also hinted at residing in Fair Oaks, California.

  • How Many Kids Do Ramit Sethi’s Parents Have?

Ramit Sethi is not the only child of their parents. There are three more of them.

There is Rachita Sethi Reddy, who according to her Facebook is a nephrologist at NYU Langone Huntington Medical Group in Huntington Station, New York. On her IG @rachitareddymd, she goes around as a double board-certified nephrologist and internist, creative mommy of 3, powerlifter, adventure fanatic, and traveler.

Rachita studied at George’s University School of Medicine before she got her board certification in Internal Medicine from the American Board of Internal Medicine. She turned 44 years old in November 2022. Also, she is married to Srinivas Haj Reddy, with three kids.

Ramit’s other sister Nagina Abdullah was born in July 1977. Professionally, she specializes in helping female professionals and executives slim down, get their dream body and keep it forever, and look amazing in pictures, on video, and on stage. Before taking on this career, she got a BA in Molecular and Cell Biology from the University of California, Berkeley, and an MBA from NYU Stern School of Business (2005 – 2007). Also, she is married to Ibrahim S. Abdullah, who like her went to NYU Stern School of Business. Besides, Nagina’s husband also studied Economics at Yale University.

Last but not least, Ramit’s brother Maneesh Sethi has been the CEO/founder of the famous Pavlok Electronics since 2013. Before this, he studied Masters in Clinical Psychology at Harvard University. As of now though he had been living around Medellín, Antioquia.
