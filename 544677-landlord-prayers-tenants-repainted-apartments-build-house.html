Landlord Says Prayers For Tenants Who Repainted His Apartments: &quot;You Will Build Your Own House&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-14
  • A man is full of joy after his tenants helped repaint his house for him, and he has shared photos of the building online
  • The landlord, Tom Alims, said the tenants were new and that he was genuinely happy because of how they made the building look clean
  • Tom shared photos of the building on Facebook and prayed that the tenants would one day build their own house too

A man is grateful to his tenants who repainted his house after renting it.

How did landlord bless tenants?

The landlord, Tom Alims, took to Facebook to share how his tenants turned his house into a more beautiful building.

Tom said the new tenants surprised him as he was not around when the painting occurred.
