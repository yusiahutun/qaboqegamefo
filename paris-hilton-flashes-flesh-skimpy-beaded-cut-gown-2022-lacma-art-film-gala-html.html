The Art+Film Gala at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art is an annual event that brings out some of the biggest names in the entertainment industry. 

This year's event honored 'artist Helen Pashgian, an influential pioneer of Southern California’s Light and Space movement, along with trailblazing Korean filmmaker Park Chan-wook,' according to the event's website. 

The proceeds from the event will help LACMA curate new 'exhibitions, acquisitions, and educational programming, in addition to screenings that explore the intersection of art and film.' 

Billie Eilish and her new beau Jesse Rutherford made it out to the event as did Kim Kardashian, Salma Hayek, Sydney Sweeney and a number of other stars as well. The event is sponsored by Gucci.
