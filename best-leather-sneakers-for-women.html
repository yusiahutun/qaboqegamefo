13 Best Leather Sneakers for Women in 2024

Publish date: 2024-03-12

Best Eco-Friendly

Veja Esplar Rubber-Trimmed Leather Sneakers

Best Eco-Friendly

Veja Esplar Rubber-Trimmed Leather Sneakers

Veja was created with exemplary ideals, focusing on eco-friendly materials, social causes, and economic justice. Traceability is of utmost importance to the founders, and it begins with the farms the bovine leather comes from and extends to the tanneries and factories the shoes are produced in. Rubber sourced from the Amazon is used for the insoles and outsoles, while trims and detailing—such as organic cotton laces and recycled EVA—are the final touches.

Sizes: 35–42 IT

Colors: White

Best Splurge

Gucci Ace Sneakers With Web

Best Splurge

Gucci Ace Sneakers With Web

A mainstay for the house of Gucci, the signature Ace sneaker is everything you’d expect from a fancy kick. Made from white leather, this lace-up style features the iconic green-and-red striped webbing—the perfect splash of excess for an otherwise minimalist shoe. Wear with everything from cotton shirtdresses in the summer to cashmere knits and trousers in the winter. We promise, this pair is worth the investment.

Sizes: 34–42 EU

Colors: White

Best for Running

Hoka Bondi SR Sneakers

Best for Running

Hoka Bondi SR Sneakers

Whether you’re looking to get your morning miles in or are in need of a run to the farmers’ market, these extra-cushioned sneakers make comfort look cool. The water-resistant leather makes them impervious to the elements, while Hoka’s signature midsole provides stability and stamina. Pair them with your favorite workout set and hoodie en route to the gym or a wide-leg jean and cropped tee for a street-style-friendly fit you can walk around in all day.

Sizes: 5–11

Colors: Black, white

Best Low-Top

Nike Air Force 1 ’07 Leather Sneakers

Best Low-Top

Nike Air Force 1 ’07 Leather Sneakers

Is there anything more iconic than the Air Force 1 sneaker? The basketball-inspired style, made with a leather upper and rubber soles, has been a shoe-closet classic since it debuted over 30 years ago. The lace-up, low-top silhouette is a little easier to pull off than the high-top for your everyday. And we love the monochromatic feel that makes this a clean styling essential. Mix with your favorite jeans, sweater, and trench coat for a simple look, or pair with a floaty, floral dress for an unexpected feminine take.

Sizes: 5–11.5

Colors: White, beige

Best Comfortable

Madewell Court Sneakers

Best Comfortable

Madewell Court Sneakers

Sneakers that look fashion-forward are not always comfortable—unless Madewell has anything to do with it. Behind the buttery exterior of these court sneakers, you will find a custom cushioned and ultra-supportive insole. The shoes were also made with reclaimed leather that otherwise would have been discarded as waste, and by a tannery that achieved a gold rating from the Leather Working Group. The outsoles are also made from recycled rubber and corn husks. A feel good purchase, for sure.

Sizes: 5–12

Colors: Ivory multi

Best Suede

Adidas Gazelle Sneakers

Best Suede

Adidas Gazelle Sneakers

By now, you know Gazelles are back, because the shoe has been regularly spotted on the likes of Kendall Jenner and Harry Styles. While you truly can’t go wrong with classic black, this reissue features metallic silver stripes instead of plain white. Easy, comfortable, and versatile, this pair feels like something you’d gravitate toward whenever you have to run out of the house.

Sizes: 5–11

Colors: Core Black, Arctic Fusion, Halo Blush, Shadow Brown, Wonder White, Grey Five

Best Minimalist

Toteme Logo-Appliqué Leather Sneakers

Best Minimalist

Toteme Logo-Appliqué Leather Sneakers

Scandi-chic brand Toteme is favored by the fashion set for its clean designs, which focus on luxe fabrications and high-quality techniques. Their leather sneaker is a minimalist’s dream, featuring a retro-inspired shape with the brand’s modern logo printed on the side. Made in Italy, these lace-up classics are destined to be worn with everything from smart tailoring to worn-in vintage denim.

Sizes: 35–42 IT

Colors: White

Best Velcro

Acne Studios Rubber-Trimmed Leather Sneakers

Best Velcro

Acne Studios Rubber-Trimmed Leather Sneakers

A favorite in Hailey Bieber’s closet, this Velcro style from Acne Studios is a unique take on the leather sneaker. In a world where lace-ups rule, the simplicity of a Velcro strap design is effortless. You can slip these on and off with major ease.

The ’80s trainer style has a rounded toe and a higher thick sole that creates traction and provides comfort. Whether styled with a sweat suit for morning coffee, or a blazer and jeans for a casual office day, these might just become your go-tos.

Sizes: 35–41 IT

Colors: White

Best Slip-On

Vans Classic Slip-On Sneakers

Best Slip-On

Vans Classic Slip-On Sneakers

Now 40% Off

Who hasn’t at one point owned the slip-on skate shoe from Vans? If you're looking for a more elevated option that still gives you nostalgia, this leather Vans is it.

Best Budget

Keds Champion Premium Leather Sneakers

Best Budget

Keds Champion Premium Leather Sneakers

A leather shoe for under $100? Count us in. The Champion sneaker style from Keds has been around for over 100 years, worn by everyone from Audrey Hepburn to Taylor Swift. But this leather version is even better than the original canvas, as it’s more resistant to spills and stains. A cushioned footbed and breathable twill lining ensure the inside feels as good as the exterior looks. Wear these from season to season with denim shorts and miniskirts in the summer, then swap for cropped jeans in the fall.

Sizes: 5–11

Colors: Off-white

Best Retro-Inspired

Rag & Bone Retro Court Sneakers

Now 60% Off

Inspired by the past, these 100 percent cow-leather court sneakers from Rag & Bone are actually quite modern. An OrthoLite footbed molds to your foot shape and gives every shoe a custom fit and feel. And the outsole is also made from recycled rubber and walnut shells.
