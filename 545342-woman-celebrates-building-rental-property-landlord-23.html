Woman Celebrates Building Her Own Rental Property and Becoming Landlord at 23

Publish date: 2024-09-03
  • A woman shared a video on TikTok documenting her journey to becoming a landlord at an age when many of her peers are still living and dependent on parents
  • The footage shows the building process of the 23-year-old's rental accommodation units
  • The video garnered attention and raised curiosity online, with some viewers curious about how she funded such a project

A determined 23-year-old woman has celebrated becoming a landlord at an age where her peers are still dependent on their parents and in a life of party after party.

Woman builds rentals

@_ndibuoshared a TikTok video showing the time, effort, and building process that went into building her rental accommodation.

The footage shows workers busy building the accommodation from the ground up and the young woman beaming with excitement as her dream becomes a reality.
