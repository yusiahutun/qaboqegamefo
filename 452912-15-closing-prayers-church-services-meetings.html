15+ best closing prayers for church services and meetings

Publish date: 2024-03-08

Whether you are going to sleep or waking up, starting a meeting or ending, beginning a church service or finishing, you pray. However, praying may seem complicated if you do not understand the context. So, what are some of the best closing prayers for church services and meetings that you can draw inspiration from?

If you take time to practise praying, you will perfect the art and become efficient. This explains why some people find it easy to pray while others don't. You could avoid the embarrassment by taking time to read through available examples. Here are some of the best closing prayers for church services and meetings that will help you out.

Closing prayer for a church service

After every church service, a closing prayer is a great way to bring about closure. Therefore, asking God for continued blessing and protection is a way to proceed. Here are closing prayer examples.

Closing prayer for a church meeting

Church meetings help increase your knowledge of gospel doctrines and principles. They also help in developing greater faith and testimony. These meetings begin and end with prayers. Here are sample closing prayers.

Father your word is a lamp to our feet and a light to our path. Thank you that we can live in Your light and walk in your truth. May the things that you have revealed and thoughts that we have shared dwell in our hearts and stir us to action. We ask all this in the precious name of Jesus. Amen!Father, thank you that you have revealed Your love to us today. We invite You to send us out from here in the power of the Holy Spirit. Fan into flame the gifts that you have given us. Come reveal Your grace and truth to us each day. For Yours is the Kingdom, the power and the glory, Forever and ever. Amen!
