Sanctity of Human Life to be celebrated Sunday | Free News

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Choices Clinic of Laurel will host its annual Sanctity of Human Life event 2-3 p.m. Sunday outside the Jones County courthouse in downtown Laurel.

Pastor Matt Olson of First Baptist Church in Sharon will offer a message about the importance of communities supporting moms, families and preborn life, organizers said.

Coffee and cookies will be served and information about Choices Clinic and how it “empowers moms to choose life and parent well” will also be available, organizers added.

Attendees should not bring signs, organizers said, as “this is a public gathering, not a rally or protest.”
