Wow!Can You Believe Carrie Underwood Won American Idol 12 Years Ago Already?

Publish date: 2024-09-06

We officially feel old!

Carrie Underwood took to Instagram on Thursday, May 25 to celebrate herself winning American Idol — and we can’t believe it’s already been 12 years since her victory!

“12 years later and I’m still not sure how it all happened to me! 12 years ago today, my dreams came true and life has been a dream ever since! I am beyond blessed and so thankful! #AmericanIdol #TBT,” the 34-year-old singer captioned a montage of snapshots from the night she won the reality singing competition.

Since her Idol days, Carrie has become one the biggest country music stars ever with six hit albums, seven Grammys, six CMAs, 11 AMAs, and 10 Billboards. She has also been married to her pro hockey player husband, Mike Fisher, since 2010, and the pair are parents to a two-year-old son, Isaiah.

‪”So happy to reunite with the amazingly talented and equally beautiful @carrieunderwood tonight. So proud of your journey,” Paula captioned the recent snap of the two.
