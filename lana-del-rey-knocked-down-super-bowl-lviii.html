Fans Noticed That Lana Del Rey Got Knocked Down Amidst Super Bowl Excitement

Publish date: 2024-09-02

There may have been a mosh pit on the field during Usher’s Super Bowl LVIII halftime performance, but there was also a mosh pit at the end of the game, up in the suite where Taylor Swift and her pals were celebrating the Kansas City Chiefs’ win. And amidst all of the excitement, one person—Lana Del Rey—ended up getting knocked into her seat, as seen in a video posted online.

In the clip posted by @PopBase, we can see the “A&W” singer standing next to rapper Ice Spice, before getting bumped over by an excited fan jumping on the other side of her. “Somebody need to go and check on Lana Del Rey… now,” one fan wrote in the replies to the post. Thankfully, the songstress appears to be laughing in the clip as she falls down, hopefully indicating that she was all right.

As the video shows, there was all kinds of enthusiasm in the suite, with everyone hugging and jumping around. One person jokingly tweeted, “Why is no one mentioning how Ice Spice got iced?” (You can see stray pieces of ice hit the the rapper as she laughs and someone beside her throws their empty cup around.) No wonder there were some casualties.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

It was a star-studded box at the NFL championship game at Las Vegas’s Allegiant Stadium, where Swift was joined by pals like Del Rey and Ice Spice, as well as Blake Lively, Ashley Avignone, Miles Teller, Keleigh Sperry, Kelce family members, and her parents.
