Guccis GG Marmont belt and shoulder bags shoot for the stars

Publish date: 2024-05-07

Featuring the brand’s Double G icon that evolved from the Running G buckle from the 70s archives, the black chevron velvet small shoulder bag is embellished with star appliqués embroidered with glimmering crystals in different colours.

The velvet belt bag with crystal star embellishment is a more retro option for bolder “it” girls. Not long ago, belt bags still weren’t considered particularly fashionable, but Gucci helped revive the retro 90s sportswear-inspired trend for young buyers. Featuring a sporty, rounded shape, the GG Marmont belt bag combines functionality with versatility – the easy-to-wear item has a specially designed leather belt so the bag can be worn high on the waist.

The star motifs on these accessories complement the soft velvet without being overly flamboyant, and the collection as a whole adds a youthful creativity to the brand’s iconic retro beauty.

Both items from the latest GG Marmont collection are available at Gucci’s newly renovated IFC store and other shops in Hong Kong and Kowloon. The price of the small shoulder bag is HK$21,000 and the belt bag is HK$15,500.
