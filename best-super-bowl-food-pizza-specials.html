25 Best Super Bowl Food Deals and Specials 2024

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Super Bowl weekend is here, and if you don't have your menu planned, it's time to get started! If cooking up a spread of Super Bowl snacks and game-day desserts is not how you'd like to spend your time, leave the cooking to the professionals.

Thankfully, knowing just how many people are going to want to focus all their attention on the big game (as opposed to, you know, cooking full meals), many restaurants and pizza joints are offering notable deals to take advantage of on Super Bowl Sunday. Whether you’re in the mood for a hot slice of ‘za, a bucket of spicy wings, or a platter of sliders, you have plenty of time- and money-saving options to choose from. Don't feel like leaving the house? Order from Uber Eats or Postmates.

The Best Super Bowl 2024 Food Deals

Applebee's

Try the endless boneless wings, Riblets, or Double Crunch Shrimp at Applebee's. You can feast and watch the game for just $14.99 a person.

Auntie Anne's

Pick up a Snack Stadium from Auntie Anne's (or have it delivered!). It includes two Pretzel Buckets filled with your choice of bite-sized pretzels and six dips in the fun stadium packaging. Don't miss the Pepperoni Nuggets!

Auntie Anne's

Bar Louie

Forget the party clean-up and dine in on game day at Bar Louie , where they're offering $3-$4 draft beers to pair with their fried pickles, nachos, burgers, and more.

Bertucci’s

With Bertucci’s Tailgate Packages, you pick up meatball or chicken parm sliders, pizza, and chicken wings to serve 10 or 20 football fans.

Bonefish Grill

Surprise the gang with a Family Bundle from Bonefish Grill. Choose from Bang Bang Shrimp Tacos, Mahi + Shrimp, or Lily's Chicken, plus salad, bread, and cookies (serves 4-5). Or head to the restaurant before the game for their Sunday brunch.

Buffalo Wild Wings

Pick up a 20/20 wing bundle from Buffalo Wild Wings to share on game day. Choose from 20 boneless wings and 20 traditional wings in your favorite sauce, plus fries, for $42.99.

Carrabba’s Italian Grill

Who said Super Bowl Sunday has to be characterized by pizza and wings? With the Carrabba's Italian Grill Family Bundles, guests can enjoy prepared meals large enough to feed four. Three of the most popular options include spaghetti, lasagne, or chicken piccata—all of which are served with bread and salad, and start at $39.99 each.

Cicis Pizza

Cicis is offering an assortment of value packs that include an array of pizzas, wings, desserts, and more, based on the size of your crowd. But it will be hard to pass up the Piezilla with 64 slices of pizza.

Domino's Pizza

Feed a crowd with one of these deals from Domino's. Choose any 2 or more items for $6.99 each from their Mix & Match Deal. Now's a great time to try their new oven-baked dips.

Robert Lachman//Getty Images

Donato's Pizza

Try the new Italian Cheese Bread or Mariachi Cheese Bread from Donato's. Both are just $6 when you order a medium or large pizza.

Fazoli's

Get a pizza and 10 boneless wings for $16.99 with code Football24 (available at participating Fazoli's locations).

Fleming's Prime Steakhouse & Wine Bar

Elevate your Super Bowl party with a party platter from Fleming's. Choose from a Filet Mignon sandwich platter, sliders, crispy chicken bites, and a variety of other appetizers, burger platters, sides, salads and more.

Hungry Howie's

Choose two items on Hungry Howie’s Mix & Match menu for $6.99 or check out the $19.99 Howie Deal, which comes with two medium one-topping pizzas and a two-liter of Pepsi. Not sure what to order? Their Pizza Party Planner calculator is here to help!

Krispy Kreme

Buy any dozen donuts from Krispy Kreme and get a second dozen for just $2. Use promo code SCORE when you order online.

Ledo Pizza

Enjoy a super spread with Ledo Pizza’s Family Feast Deal for just $38.99. The deal includes a large one-topping pizza and 12 jumbo wings.

Logan’s Roadhouse

Hoping to have a full sit-down meal on Super Bowl Sunday? Logan’s Roadhouse is offering several options for take-home feasts, starting at $69.99

Marco's Pizza

Pick up a Pizza Bowl from Marco's Pizza, or feed the whole crew with the Game Day Fest. It includes two medium one-topping pizzas, Cheezybread, and a 2-liter soda for $21.99* (use code HD189).

Moe's Southwest Grill

Moe's rewards members will get 20% off of a meal kit for Super Bowl Sunday. Choose from tacos, nachos, or fajitas.

Noodles & Company

Buy a Pan at Noodles & Company on February 11 and get 50% off dessert, side, or drink and free delivery.

Papa John's

Papa John's is giving fans the chance to win big with their Vegas Style Pizza February 2-9. Enter and read the complete contest rules on the website. (No purchase necessary, but you'll get extra entries if you order a Epic Stuffed Crust pizza.)

Popeyes

There's no better time than Super Bowl Sunday to try the new wings at Popeyes. Choose from Ghost Pepper, Sweet ‘N Spicy, Honey BBQ, Roasted Garlic Parmesan, and Signature Hot.

Potbelly

Craving a sandwich on game day? Potbelly locations are offering free deliver on orders of $20 or more when you order online or through the app.

Qdoba

This option will be a hit at any Super Bowl party. Pick up a taco kit, mega burrito pack, or a family meal from Qdoba .

Qdoba

Round Table Pizza

Use code DOUBLEPLAY and get a Large Double Play Pizza for $22.99 at Round Table Pizza.

Wing Stop

Pick up one of the Wing Group Packs to feed a crowd. Wing Stop offers packs for two (15 wings, large fries or veggie sticks, two dips, and two drinks) up to a 100-piece pack. Not sure how much to order? Check out their wing calculator.
