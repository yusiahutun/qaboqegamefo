What is Margot Robbie famous for?

Margot Robbie is an actress renowned for numerous popular movies and TV shows. She first gained recognition in Australia, her home country, after joining the cast of the long-running Australian soap opera Neighbours.

Margot became popular worldwide after participating in Martin Scorsese’s The Wolf of Wal Street (2013) and the 2017 biopic I, Tonya. The production earned her an Academy Award nomination. Margot is now an established actress and plays the role of Harley Quinn in the DC Extended Universe films.
