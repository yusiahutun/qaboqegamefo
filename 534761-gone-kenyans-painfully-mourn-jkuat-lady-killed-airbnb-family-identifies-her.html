Gone Too Soon: Kenyans Painfully Mourn JKUAT Lady Killed in Airbnb after Family Identifies Her

Publish date: 2024-07-10
  • The Muendo Mutea family has positively identified the lady who was murdered at TRM Drive in Roysambu as their daughter, Rita Waeni
  • According to the statement to TUKO.co.ke by the family, Waeni was a student at JKUAT and was to commence her fourth year
  • Before her gruesome death, Waeni used to live with her dear aunty in Syokimau, and now her family has retreated to go and mourn her as condolences from netizens continue to stream in

The identity of the lady who was brutally murdered at an Airbnb in Roysambu on January 14, 2023, has been identified as Rita Waeni Muendo.

Where was Rita Waeni Muendo studying?

According to her family, the lady was a fourth-year student at the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture Technology and lived with her aunt in Syokimau.

Waeni met her killer on Instagram

On January 14, 2024, Kenyans learnt of the brutal murder of a 20-year-old girl in an Airbnb at TRM Drive in Roysambu.

According to the police, the poor victim met her killer on Instagram, and the two hit it off and then planned to go and have a cosy time in the apartment.

The suspect had been living in Ruaka, Kiambu county, just four days before committing the heinous crime.
