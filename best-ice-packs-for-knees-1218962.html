    FAQ's

    What is the best way to put ice on your knees?

    When you are using a reusable gel or clay ice pack, place it in the freezer for the recommended amount of time. Don’t keep the ice pack directly on the affected area. Place a cloth in between to prevent frostbite and blood clots.

    How long can you leave an ice pack on your knees?

    It is usually recommended to leave an ice pack on your knees for about 15 to 20 minutes at a time, up to three to four times per day. If you leave it for longer than 30 minutes, it can cause skin damage.

    Is it okay to ice your knees every day?

    Yes. If you have swelling, pain, or inflammation, then you can apply ice several times every day.

    What is the fastest way to relieve knee pain?

    The first step of relieving knee pain is resting. Take a break from your usual activities and relax for a while. Based on the type of injury you have, select hot or cold therapy for pain relief. Cold therapy ice packs can be wrapped around the knee to reduce inflammation and swelling.

    How do you reduce inflammation in the knees?

    Applying ice packs is the best way to reduce inflammation in the knees. You can also take anti-inflammatory medication after consulting with your doctor.

    Is it better to ice or heat the knee to get rid of pain?

    This completely depends on what you intend to accomplish because heat helps loosen tight muscles and joints and relieves pain and muscle spasms, whereas ice reduces swelling, inflammation, and bruising.
