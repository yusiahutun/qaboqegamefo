How to Add or Remove Your Relationship Goals on Tinder

Publish date: 2024-02-22

The Relationship Goals feature on Tinder takes some of the guesswork out of dating, making sure that you and your potential matches have similar goals when using the app.

But how do you set your Relationship Goals on Tinder? Can you remove your Relationship Goals?

Here's how to use the feature.

How to Select Relationship Goals on Tinder

Tinder lets you choose between six relationship goals:

  • Long-term partner.
  • Long-term, open to short.
  • Short-term, open to long.
  • Short-term fun.
  • New friends.
  • Still figuring it out.

The goal you select will show on your profile alongside other bio information such as your lifestyle and interests.

To set or change your relationship goals on Tinder, follow these steps:

Close
  • Open the Tinder app and select the profile icon at the bottom right.
  • Select the edit button (pen icon) next to your profile picture.
  • Scroll down to the Relationship Goals section and select the right-facing arrow.
  • Choose between one of the relationship goals.
  • Tinder will automatically save your relationship goal selection.

    • Your dating goals will now show on your profile when users expand to see more information about you.

    How to Remove Relationship Goals on Tinder

    If you've changed your mind about showing your intentions on your Tinder bio, you can remove the field from your profile.

    To remove your relationship goals on Tinder, follow these steps:

    Close
  • Open your Tinder profile and select the edit button (pen icon) next to your profile picture.
  • Scroll down to the Relationship Goals section and select the right-facing arrow.
  • Tap on the option you already have selected, so that the app un-selects this option.
  • Press the back button on your phone or tap above the relationship goals popup.

    • Tinder will save the new settings and the Relationship Goals field will show as Empty.

    If you wish to add your dating intentions again, you can just follow the same process to add your goals. Just make sure to avoid common Tinder mistakes when setting up your profile and using the app to make sure you're getting the best matches possible.

    Make Your Intentions Clear on Tinder

    The Relationship Goals feature on Tinder makes it clear what you're looking for on the app and allows you to match with people with similar goals.

    However, you can also remove this field from your profile if you no longer want to show it.
