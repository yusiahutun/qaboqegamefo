Songs

In May 2000, Billy released his debut single “One Voice” which ranked 38 at the top 40 hits on the Billboard Hot 100. Billy became the first youngest artist to have a top 40 single on the country chart. On June 20, 2000, he released his debut album, also called One Voice at Epic Records.

The album ranked 2 at the Billboard Top  Country Albums and it was also certified double platinum in the United States. Likewise, the album also included the title track “One Voice” and singles such as “Oklahoma” and “There’s a Hero”. On October 9, 2000, Billy released another single named  “Oklahoma”  ranking at Number 33 in the Billboard Hot Country Songs top 40.

In 2000, Billy released a Christmas album titled Classic Christmas. He then released his second album Dare to Dream in 2001. And it was certified gold and was a follow-up by his singles “Elisabeth” and “She’s My Girl”. After that, he released his third album named Music Through Heartsongs on April 15, 2003.

Furthermore, again in 2005, Billy signed to Image Entertainment 2005 and released his fifth album, Everything and More. Later in 2006, he released one more album titled Billy Gilman with Image Entertainment. Billy also participated in NBC’s The Voice season 11.

Billy Gilman in The Voice 2016
