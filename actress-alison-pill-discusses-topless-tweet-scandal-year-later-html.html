One year after she accidentally tweeted a topless selfie to her 16,000 followers, Alison Pill addressed the 'embarrassing technical snafu.'

The 27-year-old starlet revealed on Tuesday night's Conan that the picture was only meant to be seen by her then-fiancé, Jay Baruchel, who was out of town.

'It's a fascinating story,' the Milk actress began, 'involving not knowing that my new BlackBerry had a touch screen and responding to somebody on Twitter like, "Oh yeah, New York was great, it's 9:30 on a Wednesday, also, apropos of nothing, here are my boobs!"'
