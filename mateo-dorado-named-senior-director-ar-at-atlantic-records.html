Mateo Dorado Named Senior Director, A&amp;R at Atlantic Records

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Mateo Dorado (Photo: LinkedIn)

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Alamo Records A&R executive and Gold Music Group founder Mateo Dorado has been appointed Senior Director, A&R at Atlantic Records. Dorado is based in New York and will report to the Co-President of Black Music, Lanre Gaba.

Dorado has been with Alamo since 2017 and has been credited with developing Rod Wave, among others. He previously worked in A&R at Miami’s Interscope Records location.

Gaba said, “Mateo has all the qualities you want in an A&R — he has incredible creative instincts, knows how to nurture talent, and is unafraid to explore new sounds. It’s rare to see someone accomplish everything he’s accomplished this early on in their career, and I know it’s just the beginning for Mateo. I look forward to watching him continue to grow while bringing exciting new artists into the Atlantic fold.”

Dorado said, “Atlantic is a label with decades of success and a place that empowered me from day one. I’m excited to work with the new wave of artists from all over the globe and cultivate an artist-to-label experience like no other.”
