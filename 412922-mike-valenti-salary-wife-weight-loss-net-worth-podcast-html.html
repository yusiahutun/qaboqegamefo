Career

Valenti started his career after graduating from Michigan University. He first stepped his foot into radio as a Mad Dog & Company producer. He also co-hosted the Spartan Tailgate Show at WVFN 730 AM in Lansing, Michigan.

In 2002, he was hired as a producer of Sportsage with Jack and Tom in the 92.7 WQTX. Later, he was hired to co-host The Sports Inferno alongside Terry Fosters on 1270 WXYT in Detroit.

On 20th April 2017, his long-time co-host and broadcaster Terry announced his retirement from the Valenti and Foster show. Terry Foster and Mike Valenti had co-hosted the show together for slightly over 13 years. Terry cited the two strokes he had suffered in 2016 as the reason behind his sudden and early retirement.
