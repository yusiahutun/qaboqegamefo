About

Tae-oh Kang is a 29 year old South Korean Actor born on 20th June, 1994 in Incheon, South Korea. His zodiac sign is Gemini

Park Eun-bin is a 31 year old South Korean Actress. Born Eun Bin Park on 4th September, 1992 in Jamsil-dong, Songpa District, Seoul, South Korea and educated at Youngpa Girls’ High School, she is famous for her roles as the young Hwangbo Seol in the KBS period series The Iron Empress, Yoon Yoo-ran in the 2013 action comedy-drama film Secretly, Greatly, Song Ji-won in the JTBC romantic mystery drama series Hello, My Twenties!, Lee Se-young in the SBS sports drama series Hot Stove League, Chae Song-ah in the SBS musical romantic drama series Do You Like Brahms?, Yi Hwi/Dam-i/Yeonseon in the KBS2 historical fiction romantic drama series The King’s Affection, Kyung-hee in the 2022 science fiction action horror film The Witch: Part 2.
