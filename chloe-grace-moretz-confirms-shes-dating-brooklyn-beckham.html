Chloe Grace Moretz Confirms She's Dating Brooklyn Beckham

Publish date: 2024-05-16

Chloë Grace Moretz and Brooklyn Beckham are officially a couple!

The Neighbors 2 star confirmed she's dating the 17-year-old son of Victoria and David Beckham on Monday's Watch What Happens Live!

"I think the more I don't make it mysterious the more people don't care, so yes we're in a relationship," Moretz, 19, revealed to host Andy Cohen during a game of Plead the Fifth.

And when asked about Brooklyn's famous parents, the actress responded, "He's a great dad, she's a great mother. They genuinely are very good parents that's what matters most, they made a pretty son."

During these past two weeks, Brooklyn has been sharing adorable photos of his girlfriend even calling Moretz his "bae" in a portrait posted on May 6.

The dating speculations first started back in 2014 when they were spotted hanging out together, even attending the Teen Choice Awards together as dates.

And most recently in mid-April, rumors circulated when Moretz and Beckham were spotted at dinner together with their families and were reportedly "inseparable all night." A source told PEOPLE, "They had a massive PDA thing going on. Chloë was stroking Brooklyn's arm, and at one point Brooklyn had his head on Chloë's shoulder. They definitely seemed like a couple."
