KiKi Layne And Ariel Stachel Were Cut From Dont Worry Darling

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Don’t Worry Darling stars KiKi Layne and Ari’el Stachel play Margaret and Ted Watkins. Regarding the widely publicized “drama” surrounding Don’t Worry, Darling, new information is being spilled!

KiKi Layne and Ari’el Stachel are now speaking out about how they were “cut from most of the movie,” which was released in US theaters last week. This is in addition to the rumored on-set conflict between director Olivia Wilde and leading lady Florence Pugh. It was recently reported that there was a “screaming match” between the two on the sets, which 40 crew members have jointly denied in a statement.

For the uninitiated, in Don’t Worry Darling, KiKi Layne and Ari’el Stachel play Margaret and Ted Watkins, the former being Alice’s (Florence Pugh) close friend who forces the main protagonist to solve the horrible truth behind the fatal Victory project.

KiKi posted a joyful video and two cute images of the gorgeous on-screen duo from the Olivia Wilde-directed movie set on Instagram. But it was Stachel’s caption that got everyone talking: “The best thing about #DontWorryDarling is that I was lucky enough to meet @arielstachel.

They cut us from most of the movie, but we thrive in real life.” Stachel sweetly commented on Layne’s IG post, “My love. You are a queen and your talent shines so brightly. I was thirsty then and I’m thirsty now.”

Ari’el Stachel, on the other hand, posted five adorable photos from the Don’t Worry Darling sets and gushed about KiKi Layne: “My favorite part of my experience in Don’t Worry Darling. This woman did phenomenal work and I was thirsty the second I met her. @dontworrydarling #dontworrydarling” KiKi responded in the comments section, “Thirsty the second I met her” Love you. Grateful
