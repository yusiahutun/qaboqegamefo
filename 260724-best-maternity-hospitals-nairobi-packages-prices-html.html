Best maternity hospitals in Nairobi: Packages &amp; prices 2022

Publish date: 0001-01-01

As soon as one discovers they are expectant, one of the questions that come to mind involves the maternity hospital to visit. When searching for a maternity facility, you can easily get lost in the numerous options available. Narrowing down your options involves finding one that has qualified medical officers, a good reputation, and affordable services, among other factors. Which are the best maternity hospitals in Nairobi today?

Your maternity hospital plays a great role in your pregnancy journey. This makes it important to choose the ideal facility at the journey's beginning.

The best maternity hospitals in Nairobi

Which hospital is best for pregnancy? Here is a look at the top 10 hospitals for maternity services in Nairobi.

10. Nairobi Hospital

Located in Upper Hill along Valley Road and Argwings Kodhek road, the Nairobi Hospital is one of the oldest healthcare providers known for excellent services in maternity. Here, the expectant woman is walked through her pregnancy right from pre-conception, during the entire pregnancy, and delivery.

The facility provides some of the best postnatal care, pediatric, and gynaecology consultations. How much is normal delivery at Nairobi hospital? The Nairobi hospital maternity package sees expectant mothers pay Ksh 90,000 for normal delivery and Ksh 190,000 for a caesarian delivery.

  • Location: Argwings Kodhek Road
  • Email: hosp@nbihosp.org
  • Telephone: (+254) 703082000

9. Avenue Hospital

The Avenue hospital is located at 1st Parklands Avenue, Nairobi, and offers some of the best maternity services that a soon-to-be mum would wish to have. These include the normal delivery package at Ksh 30,000, the ABC (Antenatal care, birth till childcare) maternity package at Ksh 130,000, and the midwife delivery package at Ksh 40,000.

The total cost of the ABC delivery package includes unlimited visits to the antenatal clinic, antenatal profile, obstetric scan, one obstetrician visit, multivitamins and ferrous sulphate supplements, delivery (whether normal, vacuum or by caesarian section), a follow up post-natal visit, the well-baby clinic for a year, and KEPI vaccines.

8. Nairobi Women's Hospital

The Nairobi Women's Hospital is a modern healthcare facility located in Hurlingham Medicare Plaza, Argwings Kodhek Road, Nairobi. It has several delivery packages with different charges.

The normal delivery package costs Ksh 30,000 and is for mothers who do not require an obstetrician. The package includes delivery room and nursing charges, ward bed for mother and baby, drugs and dressings, and initial immunization. It excludes vacuum, complicated delivery, drugs used in the induction of labour where required, doctors and caesarian section.

The normal delivery package plus an obstetrician and paediatrician (first examination) costs Ksh 40,000.

7. Coptic Hospital

The Coptic Hospital is located opposite China Center along Ngong Road. The facility should be on top of their list for women looking to deliver their babies in a faith-based medical centre.

Coptic is a fully-fledged hospital under the Coptic Orthodox Church. The hospital provides excellent care for pregnant women, including gynaecological check-ups, and antenatal and postnatal clinics. It also has maternity and pediatric facilities. The facility's maternity charges are Ksh 50,000 for normal delivery and Ksh 140,000 for a caesarian delivery.

  • Location: Ngong Road, Nairobi
  • Email: info@coptichospitals.org
  • Telephone: +254 (0) 711 043 000

6. MP Shah Hospital

The facility is located along Shivachi Road in Parklands. The services offered at the facility's antenatal and maternity clinic include gynaecology, antenatal, and postnatal care. The hospital's maternity package is subdivided into three groups: the antenatal package, the normal delivery package, and the caesarian delivery package.

The antenatal visit package comprises 8 antenatal visits with specialized gynaecologists and obstetricians with one ultrasound. It costs Ksh28,000.

The normal delivery package comprises CSSD and drugs, paediatrician charges, and other costs related to normal delivery. The cost of this package is Ksh 99,000.

The caesarean section delivery package comprises five days of admission, nursing and surgeon's fees, and anaesthetic charges. This package costs Ksh 220,000 and increases to Ksh 250,000 if the appointment is booked after 32 weeks.

  • Location: 1 Shivachi Rd, Nairobi
  • Email: info@mpshahhospital.org

5. Mater Hospital

The Mater Hospital is located in Mariakani Gardens, along Dunga Road, in Nairobi's South B area. It is one of the oldest private health facilities known for providing the highest quality healthcare services. Mater Misericordiae is primarily owned by Catholic nuns.

Mater Misericordiae is among the best maternity hospitals in Nairobi. Healthcare professionals are available to ensure you and your baby get nothing but the best. Maternity charges for normal delivery are Ksh 65,000 (with a midwife), Ksh 99,000 (with a gynaecologist) and Ksh 200,000 for caesarian section delivery.

4. Nairobi West Hospital

The Nairobi West Hospital is located in Nairobi's South C area along Gandhi Avenue, off Mombasa Road. It is well equipped with healthcare staff to ensure that every pregnant woman receives the best care during the antenatal, labour, and postnatal periods.

The cost of normal delivery is Ksh 55,000. The package includes a two-day stay in the hospital, diagnostic services, and accommodation in the general ward. The caesarian delivery package costs Ksh 168,000.

The caesarean package includes a four-day stay in the ward, theatre, surgeon and anaesthetic fee, pediatric and gynaecological reviews, immunization, medication, and consumables. Both packages include delivery by a doctor.

  • Location: Gandhi Avenue, Nairobi, Kenya
  • Email: enquiry@nairobiwesthospital.com
  • Telephone: 0730 600 000

3. Komarock Modern Hospital

Komarock Modern Hospital is one of the fastest-rising healthcare facilities. It boasts of some of the latest equipment and best service provision. The two main branches have fully equipped theatres and an intensive care unit. They also have a newborn unit, which gives one the peace of mind that their baby will be well taken care of if he/she requires specialized care.

2. Jacaranda Maternity

Located in Kahawa West, the hospital is one of the most affordable healthcare facilities in Nairobi. It provides individualized care to each mother and seeks to make the childbearing experience the best moment ever. The facility provides high-quality and affordable family health care services.

Jacaranda has one of the most affordable maternity packages in Kenya. The hospital charges are Ksh 25,000 for normal delivery and Ksh 60,000 for a caesarian delivery. NHIF covers Ksh 10,000 for normal delivery and Ksh30,000 for caesarian delivery.

1. Ruai Family Hospital

Which is the best hospital to give birth in Kenya? The Ruai Family Hospital (RFH) tops the list of the best maternity hospitals in Kenya. Located near Kangundo Road, in Ruai, opposite Ruai Mosque in Nairobi. It also has branches in several other areas around Nairobi.

The hospital is known for providing excellent maternity care. It offers early access to maternity services as it aims to improve the outcomes for pregnant women.

The RFH maternity charges are relatively affordable to most people. The charges for normal delivery are Ksh 40,000 (midwife-assisted), Ksh 60,000 (gynaecologist assisted), and Ksh 120,000 for a caesarian delivery.

  • Location: Kangundo Road Nairobi Opp. Ruai Mosque
  • Telephone: 0700 773 884
  • Email: info@ruaifamilyhospital.org

How much is the maternity fee in Kenya?

The fees vary from one hospital to another. These typically range from Ksh 20,000 for normal deliveries to more than Ksh 200,000 for caesarian section deliveries.
