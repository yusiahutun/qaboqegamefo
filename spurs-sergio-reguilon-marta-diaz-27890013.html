Spurs fans gutted Reguilon's stunning WAG Marta Diaz is leaving with the full-back

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Tottenham Hotspur fans have been left gutted at Sergio Reguilon’s departure - and it’s not out of love for the defender.

The 25-year-old Spaniard has joined Atletico Madrid on a season-long loan after struggling to cement his place in Antonio Conte’s side. The left-back made 67 appearances across two seasons for Spurs, including 22 Premier League starts last campaign.

And as he posed with his new red-and-white Atletico shirt with a no.3 on the back, fans couldn’t help but feel emotional at the player’s departure. They took to Twitter to voice their heartbreak, but appeared more heartbroken at his girlfriend Marta Diaz leaving.

One fan tweeted: “You will be sorely missed and we can’t thank you enough for your service! All the best Marta Diaz! oh and all the best Reguilon.”

A second user wrote: “The thing I hate about Reguilon transfer is that Marta Diaz will now be cheering for Atletico.”

Another added: “When Reguilon leaves but it means Marta Diaz goes with him,” with a gif of Southampton’s Ralph Hassenhutl celebrating before calming himself down.

Who do you think Spurs fans will end up missing more? Let us know in the comments section.

A fourth pointed out: "We’d get a bigger transfer fee for Marta than we would for Reguilon."

Marta was spotted looking glamorous in a figure-hugging dark brown dress and yellow heels during Reguilon’s unveiling for Diego Simeone’s side. The stunning Spaniard stole the show as usual with her glamorous attire and luscious brown locks.

The pair began dating in 2019 - after they were spotted at the wedding of Sergio Ramos and Pilar Rubio in Sevilla.

Marta herself had already shot to fame in her homeland thanks to her YouTube page - which started out as a vlogging channel in Spain. And she still makes sure to vlog, capturing her time in London as well as trips to Disneyland Paris, Ibiza and Barcelona.

The 21-year-old now has over 3.2million followers on Instagram as a result of her personality and plethora of stunning snaps. It also happens to be 1million more than the Spurs loanee himself boasts.
