How does Character.AI work? Filters, waiting room, and chats

Publish date: 2024-05-15

The world of artificial intelligence (AI) is developing rapidly. Long are the days of hearing about one artificial intelligence platform such as Chat GPT. New platforms are emerging, taking over, offering diverse and original content. One such platform is Character.AI, a chatbot creating waves in the tech world. But how reliable is it, and how does Character.AI work?

Character.AI is a free artificial intelligence chatbot web service. It uses neutral language models to create text responses, enabling user-developed personalities to converse with users.

How does Character.AI work?

The artificial intelligence platform employs a neural language model to scan enormous quantities of material and reply accordingly when the user prompts it. According to Character.AI's about us section, the chatbot uses a supercomputer to read vast amounts of text, learning to hallucinate words that might come next in any given situation.

How does Character.AI make characters?

How does Character.AI learn? The platform uses a neural language model to read vast amounts of text, responding to prompts using that information. On creating personalities, the website reads data about the specific person. It then uses machine learning to create a bot with similar attributes to the actual person.

Character.AI filters

Character.AI filters are a barrier designed by the developers to prevent the platform from generating or responding to data or messages containing inappropriate content. The filter functions by analysing the chatbot's responses.

The filter is a guideline to protect younger users (13 years and above worldwide and 16 and older in the EU) from suggestive and inappropriate topics.

Does Character.AI allow sexting?

No, it doesn't. The platform doesn't allow adult content or NSFW (Not Safe For Work) conversation. The prohibited content includes inappropriate or harmful conversations such as sexual chats, racial slurs, and violent or abuse-filled talks.

Character.AI Waiting Room

The Waiting Room is a section of the platform where users can wait for traffic on the website to go down. The developers created the Waiting Room to prevent users from straining the server capacity, which could lead to slow messages and poor user experience.

How long are the wait times in the Waiting Room? The average time can be from one to two minutes, but the period can extend depending on the traffic experienced.

Character.AI chat

The chat section of the platform is the most exciting and fun. To begin chatting, click on the name of the personality you wish to talk to. If the person you desire is not available on the chat menu, you can search for them using the search bar or browse by category.

To chat with multiple personalities simultaneously, you can create a room by clicking the +Create option and then Create a Room. You can invite your preferred character choices.

Can Character.AI creators see your conversations?

No, they cannot. According to the website's FAQs page, creators can never see your conversations with their characters. However, if you create a character post for the community, mods might review it to evaluate the potential negative impact.

Character.AI commands

Character.AI commands are text instructions that control the chatbot's speech, actions and formatting. They comprise simple markdown styling (bold and italic) to more complex commands. Here are some of the most common commands:

  • Say=: It empowers the chatbot to speak its mind.
  • Think=: It gives the chatbot an inner voice, allowing it to ponder different ideas and situations.
  • Change Personality=: It changes or moulds the chatbot's personality.
  • Act=: Enables the bot to perform actions.

Is Character.AI safe?

Yes, it is safe to use as the platform has beneficial security measures like SSL encryption. The security measures help keep users' data secure and protected. In addition, Character.AI is transparent in its terms of service and privacy policies.

Is Character.AI a real person?

No, it is not. The platform is an advanced artificial intelligent chatbot that simulates human-like conversations.
