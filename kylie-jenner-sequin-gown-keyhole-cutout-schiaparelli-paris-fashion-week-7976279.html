Kylie Jenners Plunging, Sequin-Covered Gown Featured a Literal Keyhole Cutout

Publish date: 2024-05-30

Paris Fashion Week is officially in full swing and Kylie Jenner is out to play. Just a day after arriving at Acne Studios’s Spring Summer '24 show in a fiery-red mermaid dress coupled with a set of geographically appropriate French-girl bangs, the A-lister stepped out yet again to attend Schiaparelli’s show in what may just be one of her best (and glitziest!) gowns to date.

On Thursday, Jenner was spotted heading into the show, which took place at Paris’s Italian Embassy, wearing a plunging, sequin-covered cream Schiaparelli gown that featured a backless design, a floor-skimming length, and (best of all) a literal keyhole-shaped cutout. The Kylie Cosmetics founder accessorized the shimmering dress with a cream-colored pair of the fashion house’s signature toe heels, and she kept the glitz going by adding oversized silver drop earrings to her ears.

Making a case for the return of millennial-approved side parts, Kylie wore her brunette hair down in bouncy waves complete with a voluminous, off-center part during the outing, and she rounded out her glam by adding rosy cheeks and a super glossy pink lip.

Before heading to the show, Jenner was sure to share her excitement for the outing (and her signature sense of humor) by posting a short clip of her OOTD on TikTok and Instagram.

In the video, the Kardashians star utilized a viral audio from her 2019 Met Gala appearance to mouth, “OK, let’s go family. It’s showtime! It’s fucking showtime!” while fixing her hair and snapping some pre-show photos. 

Naturally, it didn’t take long for the star’s famous friends to flood the post’s comments section with supportive messages, with Justine Skye writing, “Sickening,” and Halle Bailey keeping it short and sweet by adding a heart-eyes emoji.
