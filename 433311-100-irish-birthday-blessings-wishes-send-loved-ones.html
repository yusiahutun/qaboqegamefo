What is a traditional Irish blessing?

The Irish people have an old tradition of 'bumping' their kids by holding them upside down. The head of the child is bumped on the floor the number of times equal to the age. This ritual brings good luck to the child. If you are an adult, you can choose to hold a party with your friends or loved ones in your favourite pub.

What is an Irish birthday wish? To wish your friend or loved one a happy birthday in Irish, all you need to say is "La Breithe Shona Dhuit!"
