Mary Kilobi Stuns after Displaying Huge TV, Exquisite Living Room Decor While Watching EPL

Publish date: 2024-09-12
  • Francis Atwoli's wife, Mary Kilobi, shared a photo of their living room as she mourned Arsenal's performance in an EPL game
  • The living room spoke of opulence, given its size and tasteful decor from its ceiling to the floors
  • Many appreciated its size, with a netizen complementing Mary on using the minimalist approach in decorating her huge house

As KTN News presenter Mary Kilobi was busy mourning the loss of Arsenal in an English Premier League game, many were admiring her living room, and it is easy to see why.

Eye-catching decor in Mary Kilobi's living room

The expansive living room spoke of nothing but opulence; its breadth and height gave a lot of space, and one would be forgiven for thinking it was a hotel lobby.

Another attention-grabbing feature in the living room was the couple's giant television set in a white entertainment unit.

Both sides of the TV showed cute seats in beige, a colour that normally complements other colours when set together in the living room.

In that regard, the seats complemented the huge maroon carpet in the living room.

"Oooh my God what's going on? Wololo! Arsenal!" Mary wondered on her wall.

As some discussed the giant EPL team on her wall, others were just glued to the settings of the huge room.
