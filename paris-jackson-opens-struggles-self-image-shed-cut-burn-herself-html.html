Paris Jackson opens up about her struggles with self image and how she'd 'cut and burn' herself

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Paris Jackson has admitted to cutting herself and attempting suicide 'many times'. 

In a new installment of her Facebook Watch series, Paris, 22, talks about her struggles with self image and harming herself during her lowest points suffering from depression.

The daughter of late singer Michael Jackson said cutting herself felt like a 'distraction' from her 'emotional pain' in the second episode of Unfiltered: Paris Jackson and Gabriel Glenn Paris.

The Soundflowers singer said: 'I gained a lot of weight and it [eating] became an addiction... and then a cousin called me fat so I was like, "OK, I can't do that anymore". And that's how I fell into self-harm.

'I would cut and burn myself. I never thought that I would died from it because I was always in control of the razor and I knew how deep I was going.

'Part of it was the dopamine release. Dopamine is called dope for a reason - it feels good.

'Things like food and sex and drugs and music and working out... there's a lot of things that cause a dopamine release.'

'Yes, I tried to kill myself many times.' she confessed.

Paris tried to commit suicide when she was 15 in 2013, slitting her wrist and taking an overdose of painkillers.

Afterwards she was sent to a therapeutic boarding school in Utah, where she spent her sophomore year of high school and half of her junior year.
