You Goat Mail - Send Stuffed Goats to Friends

Publish date: 2024-02-24

After a long day, when nothing seems to be going your way, it's easy to feel down in the dumps. But imagine if you went to go check your mail, only to find that someone sent you a stuffed goat for no real reason at all. We don't know about you, but our spirits would most definitely be lifted. That's the idea behind You Goat Mail, a new service that lets you send cuddly plush toys to friends.

Why send someone a stuffed goat? YouGoatMail.com puts it best: "Everyone knows the greatest gift of all time would be a goat."  (Obviously.)

The service is completely anonymous, meaning you can surprise anyone, even if it's someone who you don't know that well, but you think needs cheering up. 

Know a distant relative who has been struggling recently? She needs a goat.

Is your grandson feeling frustrated in his classes at school? Send him a goat. 

That recent divorcée who keeps posting sad Facebook statuses? She could definitely use a stuffed goat in her life. 

Upset because no one has thought to send you a goat yet? Send one to yourself!

Each goat costs $29.99 and can be sent internationally along with a hand-written note.  

Send everyone you know a goat today at You Goat Mail.
