Hilary Duff Talks Fit Physique, Says She Doesn't Care To Look &quot;Perfect&quot; In A Bikini!

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Hilary Duff isn't stressing over her bikini bod!

The "Younger" star got candid about her fitness routine during a new interview with Today.com on Tuesday.

"I don't care to look absolutely perfect in a bathing suit," the 28-year-old told the site. "I'm a normal girl. I'm strong. I'm fit. I feel good about myself after I've had a baby."

Like most women, the actress says her weight fluctuates. "I go through ups and downs, being like, 'Damn, I'm fit right now' and 'OK, I'm letting things slide a little' and I'm totally fine with that," she confessed.

Though Duff does try to hit the gym, she also stays in shape by doing activities with her and her ex-hubby Mike Comrie's adorable son Luca.

"We love building Legos together. We love going to the park together. We play grocery store a lot," the "Sparks" singer revealed. "We scooter! I have a pink Razor scooter. So I've given up on all my coolness because he is like a speed demon on his Razor, and I can't keep up!"
