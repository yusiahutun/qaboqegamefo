Ant Anstead's biography

The famous TV host was born in Plymouth, Devon, England. He is the second born in a family of four boys. Not much is known about his family except that he has a younger brother named Michael S Anstead. He lived in Cambridgeshire until the age of ten before his family moved to Hertfordshire, where he schooled.

Education

He attended Richard Hale School in Hertford, Hertfordshire, UK. Later on, Ant joined Haileybury School, where he finished his A-levels.

Career

After completing school at the age of 18, he went for police training. Ant became a Police Constable in 1999 at Hertfordshire Constabulary and was first posted at Bishop's Stortford. He became one of the youngest armed officers in the country.

Later on, he got two commendations for bravery while working for the Hertfordshire Constabulary. However, he left the police force in 2005 in order to pursue a career in car building. He began restoring and building cars for various clients as well as building recreation cars.
