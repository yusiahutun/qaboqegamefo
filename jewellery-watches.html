20 best pearl earrings for women

Publish date: 2024-04-26

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Alighieri//Instagram

Alighieri

From Alighieri's latest collection, these statement gold earrings are handcrafted in the brand’s Hatton Garden studio: cast in recycled bronze then plated in 24-karat gold. We love how all of the brand's freshwater pearl pendants are totally unique.

Kiki McDonough

Kiki McDonough

Kiki McDonough's jewellery is firm favourite of the royal family – the Princess of Wales has been seen in an impressive 21 different pieces from the British jewellers. Cast from 18-karat white gold, this pair is defined by oversized pearl pendants suspended from diamond-encrusted studs. Show yours off with a low-sweeping bun or off-the-shoulder necklines.

Chanel Pre-Owned

Chanel Pre-Owned

We all know that shopping for pre-owned pieces is one of the best ways to stay sustainable when it comes to your fashion choices, so why should pearls be any different? A vintage 1995 find, these clip-on Chanel drop earrings feature the brand’s signature interlocking CC logo and an oversized faux-pearl. Shop other second-hand items here.

Mizuki

Mizuki's 14-karat gold hoops delicately thread through the lobe and are finished with a dainty freshwater pearl. Wear yours with tousled up-dos to let the sculptural design really sing.

Chopard

From Chopard's aptly-named Happy Hearts collection, these rose-gold earrings are defined by their floral design – five heart-shaped petals set with delicate mother of pearl – and finished with the brand's iconic floating 0.08 carat diamond at the centre.

Bvlgari

You can always trust the luxury Italian jewellery brand Bulgari to put an abstract spin on classics like the pearl stud. Cast from 18-karat gold, these striking earrings are set with fan-shaped mother of pearl and dotted with 0.07 carats of diamonds. The pearl inlays are designed to symbolise the curved marble architecture of the quintessentially Italian Caracalla Baths in Rome.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Sophie Billie Brahe

Sophie Billie Brahe

Nobody knows pearls quite like Sophie Billie Brahe. The jewellery designer is one of the few to experiment with the stone’s lesser-known colours; these earrings feature both creamy, iridescent and rose-pink pearls. The double-drop cluster design is inspired by the early renaissance artist Botticelli, whose Birth of Venus painting is one of fashion’s favourite inspirations (Dolce & Gabbana made an entire print based on it back in the 1990s).

Kitty Joyas

Kitty Joyas

Founded in Barcelona, jewellery label Kitty Joyas champions slow, considered design. Featuring gold-plated, enamel-set studs that drop to freshwater baroque pearls, the brand's boho earrings will make perfect additions to your capsule wardrobe.

Pandora

Perfect for the modern woman, Pandora’s earrings are simple, contemporary and classic all at the same time. Cast from sterling silver, these hoops drop to a baroque freshwater cultured pearl – each one is totally unique.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Van Cleef & Arpels

Van Cleef & Arpels

From the coveted Alhambra jewellery collection first created in 1968, these four-leaf clover earrings are a much-loved signature for Van Cleef & Arpels. We love how the mother of pearl catches the light beautifully with every turn of the head.

Simone Rocha

Simone Rocha

Now 50% Off

Pearls are a much-loved signature of Simone Rocha – the designer uses the semi-precious stone throughout her headbands, hair slides and earrings. This pair features graduating strands of faux pearls and is finished with delicate ivory ribbons.

Missoma

If you’re looking for an eccentric piece to accessorise with, Missoma's coveted collaboration with the fashion designer Harris Reed is just the ticket. The addition of black crystals adds a touch of grunge to these delicate white pearl and recycled gold-vermeil studs.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Mateo

If you want to make a jewellery statement, Mateo's wearable works of art will do nicely. These eye-catching crescent moon earrings are studded with 0.92 carats of diamonds and feature three rows of cascading pearls.

Pomellato

You can always trust the luxury Italian jewellery brand Pomellato to put an abstract spin on classics like the pearl stud. Cast from 18-karat rose gold, these striking earrings are set with spherical Tahiti pearls (beloved for their unique grey hue) and set with 1.90 carats of diamonds. The jacket clasp and pavé stones add a vintage feel.

Annoushka

The Princess of Wales has been spotted wearing Annoushka's pearl earrings countless times since she first invested in a pair back in 2012. Proving that pearls are the definition of versatile, Kate has worn them with everything from tailoring and T-shirts, to lace dresses and pillbox hats, and even used them to elevate a navy fleece at the America's Cup Regatta. This season, the brand has updated its best-selling 18-karat gold and freshwater pearl style with 26 sparkling brown diamonds.

Monica Vinader

Monica Vinader

Mejuri

Mejuri’s elegant earrings have a mix-and-match twist with two pearls: a stud that sits on the ear and one that cradles the lobe. The ear jacket can also be detached to create a simpler, everyday look.

Dior

Featuring white resin pearl pendants dropping from that iconic CD monogram, these gold-tone Dior earrings will bring a playful touch any outfit. Go all out with the matching bracelet, too.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Olivia & Pearl

Olivia & Pearl

Featuring graduated freshwater pearls set in recycled silver, Olivia & Pearl earrings are truly timeless. Complement this classic drop design with a sleek up-do for a striking evening look.

Boodles

Iconic British jeweller Boodles' timeless pearl earrings are defined by two oversized cultured pearls and mirrored, 1.39 carat diamond 'B' motifs. A different take on the traditional drop pearl earring, this design delicately cradles the back of the ear.
