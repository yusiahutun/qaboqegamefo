Kendall Jenner, Elle Fanning Lead the Way at L'Oral's Star-Studded Paris Show

Publish date: 2024-03-27

Paris Fashion Week was lit with a Hollywood glow as L’Oréal debuted its star-studded runway show this Sunday on the esplanade of the Eiffel Tower.

The sixth edition of the Le Défilé Walk Your Worth show highlighted the synergy between true beauty and high fashion with a celebrity lineup that included Kendall Jenner, Elle Fanning, Eva Longoria, Dame Helen Mirren, and more.

Fanning looked goddesslike in a plunging golden gown covered in beads and sequins. She wore the piece with strappy golden sandals and had her hair styled in silky Old Hollywood waves. The star of The Great sweetly held hands with Mirren as the two walked down the runway together.

Mirren looked fabulous in a striped silver sequin gown with long sleeves that turned into trains and flowed behind her as she walked. The dress was cinched at the waist with a ribbon belt, and the actor wore it with golden sandals. Mirren’s hair was styled in a glamorous ’70s ’do, and she wore subtle silver eye shadow.

Arnold Jerocki//Getty Images

Kendall Jenner—the new face of the iconic French cosmetics brand—closed the show with a theatrical runway walk. Looking striking in a silver glitter gown, she was flanked by dancers in all-black clothing, who danced around her as she walked. The supermodel swished her long, shiny ponytail and smiled and waved at the audience.

Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

Longoria, Andie MacDowell, Viola Davis, and Bollywood legend Aishwarya Rai Bachchan also made welcome appearances at the show, all in their own shimmering gowns.
