New metal door designs and ideas for beauty and security

Choosing the right door for your home can be as challenging as building a home. Besides serving the practical purpose of home security, doors enhance the overall aesthetic appearance of residential spaces. Metal door designs are some of the most preferred doors by homeowners due to their durability and low maintenance.

1. Wrought metal door design

This is perhaps the most beautiful type of metal door to try this year. The door can be fully wrought or just have some inserts. You can keep it dark to achieve an impressive effect.

2. Aluminium safety doors with auspicious symbols

The main entrance tells stories about your home. So, based on your preferences, you can opt for an entrance steel door design with important symbols for you or your family.

3. Steel door design with glass inserts and translucent panels

Make your doors modern and stylish by incorporating glass inserts or translucent panels. This door design allows natural light while maintaining privacy. Glass inserts can be clear, frosted or textured, offering various levels of transparency.

The blending of steel and glass creates a sleek and sophisticated look, instantly elevating your home's exterior. Moreover, the interplay of light and shadow adds a touch of drama, making your steel door design more captivating.

4. CNC metal safety door design

Add instant flair to your home exterior with CNC metal safety doors. The above design is among the metallic door designs that are long-lasting and impenetrable. Its thin railing makes it challenging to access the inner bolt, enhancing the security.

5. Steel sliding door

This is an American-style stainless steel sliding door that can enhance the beauty of your interiors while keeping intruders at bay. They are among the latest steel doors in Kenya. It is operated on a top rail, seamlessly closing with an easy push. The sturdy steel frame has glass panels for better ventilation and visibility.

6. Steel door with traditional panel design

The classic panel design is elegant and sophisticated. Choosing a steel door with conventional panel designs will quickly improve the visual attractiveness of your home's entrance.

Whether you prefer a single or multiple-panel format, these home designs offer balance and harmony. The raised or recessed panels give the door depth and character, creating a focal point that welcomes guests with a sense of nostalgia.

7. Rustic steel door design with decorative grills and wrought iron accent

If you are a rustic and vintage vibes fan, steel door designs with decorative designs are the perfect choice. These designs have intricate patterns like floral floral patterns that bring that old-world charm to your home. The grills and patterns help in providing an additional layer of security.

8. Commercial steel door designs

Commercial steel door designs are some of the simple metal door designs in Kenya. A double-hinged door opens wide to allow vehicles and materials into the area. It is designed for commercial spaces to provide additional safety and security. They are mainly used in godowns and storehouses.

9. Minimalistic steel door design with clean lines and a sleek finish

Are you looking for a steel door design for home security? Consider this steel design with clean lines and a sleek finish. These designs embrace simplicity and minimal ornamentation, seamlessly integrating modern architectural styles.

10. Nature-inspired etched or laser-cut steel door design

This beautiful design is an elegant and timeless addition to any home, especially for nature enthusiasts. Most of these doors feature intricate patterns of trees, flowers, leaves or wildlife, creating a captivating and artistic entrance.

Nature-inspired steel doors bring a sense of serenity and connection with the outdoors. The precision of etching and laser-cutting also allows for intricate details and a personalised touch.

12. Ornate steel door design

Choose a steel door design that features elegant scrolls and archways. These doors ooze elegance and majesty, drawing inspiration from Mediterranean architecture. The scrolls and arches provide a sense of intricacy and romanticism to the entrance, making it warm and friendly.

A bronze or copper finish instantly elevates the Mediterranean vibe. Pair the door with a lush and vivid flower garden in the front yard to create a distinctive entryway.
