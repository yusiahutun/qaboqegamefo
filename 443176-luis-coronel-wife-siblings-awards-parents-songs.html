Career

Luis started singing while he was still a young boy. He joined a local church choir to hone his vocal skills. However, he didn't think of becoming a singer because he was solely focused on boxing. Instead, he began his singing career while still boxing.

After winning one of his boxing matches, Coronel sang a song to his rumoured girlfriend and posted it on social media. His fans urged him to make more music which he agreed to. Eventually, he was noticed by a major music production company called Del Records.

Luis started his career as a professional musician when he was only 16. He was signed by Del Records, where he released his debut album, Con la Frente en Alto in September 2013. The album climbed the Regional Mexican Albums Billboard chart in three weeks to reach the top spot.

Later, the album received the gold certification from the Recording Industry Association for selling 30,000 copies in Latin. He released his second album in 2016 titled Quiero Ser Tu Dueno. The album reached position one on the Top Latin Albums chart.
