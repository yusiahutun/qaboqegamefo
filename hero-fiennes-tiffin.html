Hero Fiennes Tiffin Bio, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Hero Fiennes Tiffin is an English actor who is famous for playing the role of the 11-year old version of Tom Riddle in Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince and being cast as Hardin Scott in the film After. He is signed with the modeling agency Storm Management.

Early life

Hero Fiennes Tiffin was born on November 6th, 1997 in London, the United Kingdom to film director Martha Fiennes and cinematographer George Tiffin. He has an older brother, Titan Nathaniel, and a sister, Mercy Jini Willow. He went to Raey Primary School in Lambeth, then studied at Emanuel School in Battersea, and finally completed his education at Graveney School in Tooting.

Hero Fiennes Tiffin Career

The hero was born into a family full of well-known people from the entertainment industry. He was already gifted with talent and has is always seen putting up a lot of dedication and passion to whatever role he has is assigned for. In 2008 he debuted with the film Bigga Than Ben, based on a Russian novel where he played the role of Spartak. The next year he was selected for the role of 11-year-old Voldemort from the hit film series Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince. He was specially cast for the role as his uncle, Ralph Fiennes played the original role of Lord Voldemort thus the directors felt they shared the same resemblance and would be perfect for the job.

Widespread Fame

Hero did have a well-established name in Britain but his major career breakthrough was seen when he was cast as a lead in an American romantic drama “After” where he played the role of a college boy named Hardin Scott along with Josephine Langford. This movie was released in 2019 and gained worldwide recognition. The same year he was seen in television drama series named Cleaning Up playing as Jake. He was named the face of Ferragamo made by Salvatore Ferragamo S.p.A. in November 2019.
