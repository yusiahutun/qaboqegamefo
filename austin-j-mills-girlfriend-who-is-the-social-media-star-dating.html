Austin J Mills Net Worth

Austin J Mills flaunted a net worth of above $3 million in 2022.

This millionaire first stepped into the starlight doing basketball trick shots during his senior year at Baylor University. After going viral, he was then invited to host NBA and everything snowballed from there.

But Austin never could make it to the NBA himself. However, being told he wasn’t athletic or tall enough to play at the college level, he definitely outworked those with more talent to have earned a place at Baylor University, one of the Top 25 teams in the nation.

Reportedly, Austin played walk-on guard for Baylor with 99 minutes over 17 games in his first year after transferring from Pepperdine prior to the 2014-15 season. Whereas at Pepperdine, he played games over two seasons averaging 2.4 points, 0.7 rebounds, and 0.7 assists in 9.8 minutes per game.

Besides being an athlete, Austin was also an entrepreneur at heart. During high school, he started a clothing line called FrostBite Los Angeles which sold T-Shirts and Sweatshirts. It was an immediate hit around school and the area.

Also, around the same time, he created an event planning company with two partners called JAM Events.

After his basketball career, Austin then stepped into the entertainment world and started working as a TV host at NBA Entertainment Inc ever since Oct 2016.

Don’t be surprised but Austin’s a DJ as well. He’s been DJing ever since middle school (started out DJing kids’ parties and events). His DJ name was back them DJ AYO. Now, he goes by the name DJ Mills. So far, he’s performed for a few stars like Sean Kingson and 50 Cents among many.

His dream job is to be a food host someday — “kind of like Guy Fieri”, he shared.
