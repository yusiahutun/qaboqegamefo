Pink Wears See-Through Banana-Print Bikini on Vacation With Husband

Publish date: 2024-07-29

This week, Pink gave fans a rare and unexpected glimpse into her private life with husband Carey Hart—as well as a look at her eccentric style.

The “Just Give Me a Reason” singer shared an Instagram post Tuesday featuring photos from a recent vacation she took with Hart. In the first snap, she is seen taking in the sun in a clear, strapless bikini top with a yellow banana print. She smiles and shields her eyes while posing in the see-through piece, which she paired with a silver necklace and a string bracelet.

In the next photo, Pink is seen standing in an outdoor shower, next to a tent, completely naked. She lifts up her arms and smiles widely at the cameraman—her husband. For Instagram, she censored the photo with some colorful doodles.

“Lake weekends and a new ridiculous bathing suit. If you haven’t showered outdoors while your husband tries to scare you every five minutes you haven’t lived!!!!!! #embarassingmoms #eyerollsfordays #bananaboobies #imfun #readyfortour,” the pop star captioned the post.

Pink has been married to Hart since 2006. The pair share two kids: Willow and Jameson. Their low-key couple’s getaway comes ahead of the singer’s Summer Carnival and Trustfall tours.

Summer Carnival’s North American leg will begin in Toronto on July 24. The 15-city North American Trustfall Tour will kick off October 12 with a show in Sacramento. A date in Sunrise, Florida, has been added since Pink announced the news of her ninth studio album tour in February, following the album drop.

Trustfall includes hits like lead single “Never Gonna Not Dance Again” and the title track, as well as “When I Get There,” a touching ballad Pink dedicated to her late father. It also features collaborations with Chris Stapleton, the Lumineers, and First Aid Kit.
