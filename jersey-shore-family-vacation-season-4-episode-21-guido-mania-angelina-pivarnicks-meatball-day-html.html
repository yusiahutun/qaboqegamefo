'Guido Mania' and Angelina Pivarnick's 'Meatball Day'

Publish date: 2024-06-11

The roommates have finally cleared their lives of drama in Jersey Shore: Family Vacation Season 4. They have a blast together in the season 4 episode “Guido Mania.” Angelina Pivarnick‘s husband reveals details about the woman he previously posted about on social media, Zack “24” Carpinello brings the Guidos into his world of wrestling, and Pivarnick has a “Meatball Day” of her own. 

[SPOILER ALERT: Spoilers ahead for Jersey Shore: Family Vacation Season 4, Episode 21.]

Nicole ‘Snooki’ Polizzi still hasn’t made her comeback on ‘Jersey Shore: Family Vacation’

Earlier this year, Jersey Shore: Family Vacation fans learned Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi will make her triumphant return to the MTV series in season 4B. But so far, Polizzi has only been part of Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino’s sober-versary. 

Given the footage from the teaser trailer for season 4B, we know “Snooki” shows up in the Poconos at some point. But she didn’t make it for the wrestling show Carpinello organizes in “Guido Mania.”

Chris Larangeira admits to trolling Angelina Pivarnick on Instagram 

Pivarnick’s marriage to Chris Larangeira has been a topic of conversation in season 4 of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation. After briefly moving into his mother’s house, Larangeira is back living with Pivarnick and they’re working on things. In “Welcome to the Poconos,” he agrees to go on the trip with Pivarnick. 

During their separation, Larangeira and Pivarnick both actively trolled each other on social media. In “Guido Mania,” fans learn one of Larangeira’s posts was a picture of Pivarnick — but she didn’t know it. 

“Living my best life,” Larangeira wrote over the faces in the photo he shared to his Instagram Stories earlier this year. Like Pivarnick, much of the world thought Larangeira was out with another woman. But he wasn’t. 

‘Jersey Shore: Family Vacation’ cast wrestling personas in ‘Guido Mania’

Thanks to Jenni “JWoww” Farley’s fiancé, Sorrentino gets to live out his childhood dreams and participate in a wrestling match. Carpinello spends the afternoon training Sorrentino, Vinny Guadagnino, Ronnie Ortiz-Magro, and Pauly DelVecchio on safely performing in the ring. 

Each of the roommates creates a wrestling persona for their match. Naturally, Guadagnino becomes the “Keto Torpedo.” Meanwhile, Ortiz-Magro is “Ronpage;” Sorrentino is “The Situation;” and DelVecchio is “DJ Pauly D.” Together, they make the World Guido Entertainment (WGE). 

Deena Cortese’s husband, Chris Buckner, and Pivarnick’s husband also play a role in Carpinello’s “Guido Mania.” Buckner is the referee and Larangeira is the Guido’s manager. 

Later, Guadagnino’s Uncle Nino arrives to serve as the announcer for the event. Fans will have to tune in to Jersey Shore: Family Vacation next week to see how the wrestling match plays out. 

Angelina Pivarnick and Nikki Hall have a ‘Meatball Day’ 

While Cortese and Farley take their kids to play, Pivarnick makes her own fun at the sports bar in their mountain resort. Fortunately, Delvecchio’s girlfriend Nikki Hall is there to keep her entertained and be her “DD.” 

Pivarnick bonded with Hall when she appeared in Double Shot at Love Season 2. The duo spends even more time together in the Jersey Shore: Family Vacation episode “Guido Mania.” 

“Someone’s gotta drink for 10 on vacations lol,” Pivarnick tweeted during the episode. And drink she did! 

Once again, Hall and Pivarnick talk about their relationships. As Pivarnick has expressed previously, she admires Hall’s relationship with DelVecchio. “You gotta stay off social media,” Hall reiterates to Pivarnick regarding posting about her marriage online.

Tune in next week for another all-new episode of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation Thursdays on MTV.
