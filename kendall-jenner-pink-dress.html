Kendall Jenner just wore the tiniest pink mini dress

Publish date: 2024-04-02

Kendall Jenner's latest outfit had her looking like a real-life Barbie doll. The supermodel – who is currently in Miami – stepped out for a girl's night out in the city wearing a bright pink strappy dress with a zebra print.

The mini dress is from affordable label For Love And Lemons and is still available to buy for under £200. She paired the frock with some glitzy party shoes from buzzy label Amina Muaddi (who just teamed up with Rihanna) and wore her hair in a half-up style.
