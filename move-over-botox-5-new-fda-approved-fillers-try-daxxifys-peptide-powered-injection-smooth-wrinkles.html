Here’s everything you need to know:

1. Daxxify: a cutting-edge contour tool

Daxxify, the brainchild of Revance Therapeutics, is an aesthetic injection aiming to temporarily improve frown lines and wrinkles. Powered by peptide technology, it can effectively block the nerve signal that prompts the muscles to contract, weakening the muscle and ultimately smoothing out existing wrinkles while preventing the formation of new ones.

But what truly makes Daxxify a game-changer is its extended duration of action. Injectable enthusiasts have often voiced their frustration with the short-lived effects of neuromodulators like Botox, which typically only last a few months. However, Daxxify offers results that can last between six to nine months.

2. RHA Redensit: the rejuvenation revolution

As we age, our skin gradually loses its elasticity and firmness. Enter RHA Redensit, a dermal filler that harnesses the power of hyaluronic acid. This innovative formulation adapts to your facial expressions, ensuring natural-looking results that beautifully restore volume and revitalise tired skin.

With its potential to reduce moderate to severe dynamic wrinkles for up to 12 months, it offers impressive results that last longer than other injectables, providing a promising solution for those seeking a rejuvenated and radiant appearance.

3. Topilase: topical potion for filler enhancement

If you’ve ever undergone hyaluronic acid (HA) filler injections, you’re likely familiar with hyaluronidase – the enzyme used to dissolve HA gels. Topilase is a hyaluronidase alternative made of a complex blend of enzymes – hyaluronidase, protease, and lipase – that effectively break down hyaluronic acid chains.

Complemented by five soothing botanicals, the formula is designed to be brushed onto the skin and gently massaged in – no needles required.

It acts as the final touch, helping patients achieve the most natural-looking and flawless results. One of the remarkable benefits of Topilase lies in its effectiveness on thin-skinned areas, including the lips and under-eyes. These delicate areas often exhibit irregularities or overfilling, leaving patients seeking subtle corrections.

4. PLLA fillers: the collagen catalyst

We all know that collagen is the secret to plump, supple skin, but PLLA fillers take things to the next level, stimulating collagen production from within. These injectables go beyond mere temporary plumping, working harmoniously with your body to gradually restore lost volume and rejuvenate your features.

These bio-stimulatory dermal fillers, also known as poly-L-lactic acid fillers, are synthetic products designed to restore volume loss in the face and body. Unlike traditional fillers, PLLA fillers act as stimulators rather than instant plumpers, stimulating the production of new collagen in the skin. As collagen stimulation takes effect in the treated area, patients can expect gradual and progressive results over several months.

5. Seventy Hyal 2000: the fountain of hydration

This hydration powerhouse is packed with high concentrations of hyaluronic acid, delivering intense moisture to combat dryness and plumping your skin to reveal a dewy, radiant complexion.

Designed to address both skin texture and laxity, this treatment delivers a surge of moisture while stimulating the production of new collagen and elastin. The high molecular weight of the hyaluronic acid (HA) molecules ensures deep penetration, delivering a boost of hydration and restoring the skin’s natural resilience.
