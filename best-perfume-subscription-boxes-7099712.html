The 7 Best Perfume Subscription Boxes of 2024

Publish date: 2024-02-19

Every so often, you may find yourself paying through the nose for perfumes — only to be left feeling dissatisfied with the scent after just a few spritzes. That's why we're big fans of perfume subscription boxes, where you can try samples of the most popular fragrances on the market — for a small monthly price — before committing to a full-size bottle. These boxes are the perfect way to find your signature scent.

With so many perfume subscription boxes on the market, it can be a daunting task to pick the right one, so we did the hard work for you. We evaluated these boxes for affordability, clean ingredients, and scent variety to present you with the best perfume subscription boxes.

Our Top Picks

Why It’s a Top Pick

Scentbird takes the cake for the best perfume subscription service because it blends value, convenience, and variety. For $16.95 per month, the brand offers an 8-milliliter sample of a fragrance, which should last you about 120 sprays. That’s just the beginning plan, too — you can opt for a multi-piece plan and get up to three samples for $37 per month. Bear in mind that some fragrances may be listed as “premium,” which means that you could get charged $5, $10, or $15 on top of your monthly plan.

With picks from niche and designer brands, Scentbird features an impressive library of over 600 scents, and you can choose what ships. You even get a free sleek perfume case with your first fragrance, but you’ll have to pay for any extra future cases.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What Brands Do You Get in a Perfume Subscription Box?

    You can get a wide variety of brands from a perfume subscription box. Often, you can choose from different brands, which usually include designer, celebrity, and niche ones. Other times, the subscription service picks out a couple of fragrances of the month for you to sample. Olfactif, for example, provides recommendations for indie and high-end luxury brands. Some subscription boxes, such as Skylar Scent Club, also send out limited-edition perfumes from their own brand. 

  • Do Perfume Subscription Boxes Come With Full Bottles?

    Full-size perfume subscriptions are not common. In most cases, monthly perfume subscription boxes send samples in atomizers or rollerballs with eight to 10 milliliters of fragrance. After trying out a particular perfume, you may be able to get a full-size bottle (typically 30, 50, or 80 milliliters) with a membership discount. 

  • Does Perfume Expire?

    Perfumes last about two years, but proper storage can help extend their shelf life. When you spray your perfume, air can get into the bottle and oxidize the fragrance. Oxidation can change the color of the fragrance, turning it a darker hue. It can also alter the smell and make it more sour or metallic. Perfumes with green, citrusy, patchouli, and fruity notes are particularly sensitive to oxidation, so you want to keep an eye out for these scents.

  • Where Should You Store Your Perfume?

    Exposure to direct light, heat, and humidity can degrade a fragrance. To keep your perfume fresh, place it in a dark, cool, and dry space. If possible, it’s a good idea to store your fragrance in its original box to protect it even further. Additionally, remember to recap your perfume after every use to limit its exposure to oxygen. 

  • How Can You Make Your Perfume Scent Last Longer?

    One way to make your perfume scent last longer throughout the day is by spraying your pulse points on your wrists, inside your elbows, behind your ears, behind your knees, and on the nape of your neck. These areas give off heat and can make your fragrance develop faster. It also helps to spray on moisturized skin, as fragrance sticks onto moisturized skin better than dry skin.
