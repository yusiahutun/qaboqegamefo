What kind of hair do you use for kinky twists?

Kinky twists utilize Marley hair; however, they tend to have a smaller thickness when compared to their counterparts. This means that the hair used here is more coarse. That is why it offers better longevity and numerous styling options that you can recreate to suit your needs.

Do kinky twists damage your hair?

Kinky twists are meant to protect your hair from breakage and give it enough space to grow undisturbed. However, they utilize a lot of weight pulling your hair down.

Therefore, if you have weak hair, it might not be a great option to choose. Also, when you are getting the style, ascertain that your hair is not damaged as it will make the situation worse.
