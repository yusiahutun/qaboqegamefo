Travis Scott furious over ex Kylie Jenner's relationship with Timothe Chalamet

Publish date: 2024-02-20

Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet's very public display of affection at this year's Golden Globes has irked her ex Travis Scott, who, according to insiders, believes she was trying to 'get to him.'

The new couple were captured sharing kisses and sitting hand-in-hand throughout Sunday night's ceremony at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles.

Travis, 32, who split from Kylie, 26, in January, was reportedly left 'agitated' at seeing them together as he 'does his best to avoid' being reminded of his ex's new romance.

‘Travis is agitated anytime he sees Kylie with him,' an insider told DailyMail.com exclusively. 'His feelings about them together are known already and he has addressed it in his music. 

Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet struggled to keep their hands to themselves at Sunday night's Golden Globes

The pair were captured showering each other in kisses, something that reportedly irked her ex-boyfriend - and the father of her two children - Travis Scott 

Travis, 32, believes his ex Kylie, 26 - seen together in 2022 - was trying to 'get to him' with her Golden Globes PDA, according to a source 

'He doesn’t like to see them together and he does his best to avoid it even though he could not avoid it today. 

‘Travis believes she downgraded. Of course he thinks that she is trying to get to him.'

They claimed Travis, who has daughter Stormi, five, and son Aire, one, with Kylie, is focusing on his upcoming album Jackboyz 2 and is trying to 'block out all the other noise.' 

'He knows him and Kylie are done for good and does not want to be around the two of them together,' the source continued. 'He is actively involved with his kids and always will be there for them.

'He has moved on too though and has no shortage of women to choose from.’

Timothee, 28, walked the red carpet alone on Sunday night, before he was joined by the reality star-turned-business woman inside, where they were captured engaging in a series of intimate moments on the telecast.

An onlooker told DailyMail.com: 'They were at their table staring at one another. They didn't talk to anyone during the commercial break, they were just kissing... it looked like puppy dog love, like high school.' 

News of Kylie and Travis' split broke in January 2023, just months before she reportedly started dating Timothee.

Timothee was flying solo on the red carpet on Sunday night (pictured) before he joined his girlfriend inside the Beverly Hilton 

The couple, pictured at the WSJ Magazine Innovator Awards last year, have been dating since early 2023

The couple put on a loved up display at the US Open in September last year 

Travis has not shied away from making public his apparent disdain for the new relationship, and appeared to diss Timothee in his track Meltdown back in July.

In a nod to new film Wonka, in which the actor plays the eccentric chocolatier, Travis raps: 'Chocolate AP and chocolate the Vs, got the Willy Wonka factory / Burn a athlete like it's calories, find another flame hot as me.'

A source previously told the Mail that Travis thought the romance was a 'joke.' 

'Travis is still involved in her life because of the children, but he does not think that it's a good match at all and told her it will not last,' they said last June.

Sparks between the two ignited around four months after her relationship with the controversial rapper fizzled. They are believed to have met at the Jean Paul Gaultier Fashion Show in Paris in January.

It marks Kylie's first since her final split with Texas-born rapper Travis. 

Kylie's on-again, off-again relationship with Travis began in 2017 after they met at Coachella, where the rapper was performing. 

One year later, they welcomed their daughter, Stormi. They split for the first time in 2019, but rekindled their romance in May 2021. 

Travis is reportedly trying to focus on his new music and his two children, Stormi, five - pictured with him in 2021 - and son Aire, one 

Reveal: One year prior to her split with Travis, on February 6, 2022, Kylie used Instagram to reveal their son Aire's arrival 

Their son Aire was born 10 months after they got back together, on February 6, 2022. She revealed his birth on Instagram, with a sweet black-and-white photo of his tiny hand. 

Aire's original name was Wolfe, but the former couple legally changed his name in January last year.

In legal documents they said that they 'regretted' the initial name choice, insisting that Aire is a 'better fit.'

'We just didn't feel like it was him. Just wanted to share because I keep seeing Wolf everywhere,' Kylie said about the name change. 

Their final split came in December amid rumors that Travis cheated on Kylie. It was not the first time that he had reportedly strayed, but the rapper has never admitted to infidelity.
