50 best Christian wedding wishes and messages with Bible verses

Publish date: 2024-08-29

It can be hard to find the best Christian wedding wishes to give your friends who have invited you to share their special day. Knowing what you should write on the card can be stressful, even for the most creative people.

It is important to send wedding wishes to help spread the love of God and the message of Christianity to newlyweds. Christian wedding wishes remind the couple that they are not alone in their faith and that you are rooting for their union.

Christian wedding wishes with Bible verse

Sending Christian wedding wishes with Bible verses can help bring the couple closer to God. It serves to remind them of the importance of God in their lives and, importantly, in their marriage. Here are some of the Christian wedding wishes with Bible verses:

  • Love is patient; love is kind. It does not envy; it does not boast, it is not proud. It does not dishonor others, it is not self-seeking, it is not easily angered, and it keeps no record of wrongs. Love does not delight in evil but rejoices with the truth. It always protects, always trusts, always hopes, and always perseveres. Love never fails. But where there are prophecies, they will cease; where there are tongues, they will be stilled; where there is knowledge, it will pass away. 1 Corinthians 13:4-8
  • And now, these three remain faith, hope, and love. But the greatest of these is love. 1 Corinthians 13:13
  • Do everything in love. 1 Corinthians 16:14
  • Be completely humble and gentle; be patient, bearing with one another in love. Ephesians 4:2
  • Above all, love each other deeply, because love covers a multitude of sins. 1 Peter 4:8
  • There is no fear in love. But perfect love drives out fear because fear has to do with punishment. The one who fears is not made perfect in love. We love because he first loved us. 1 John 4: 18-19
  • Hatred stirs up conflict, but love covers all wrongs. Proverbs 10:12
  • Whoever does not love does not know God because God is love. 1 John 4:8
  • Love must be sincere. Hate what is evil; cling to what is good. Romans 12:9
  • Husbands love your wives, just as Christ loved the church and gave himself up for her. Ephesians 5:25
  • However, each one of you also must love his wife as he loves himself, and the wife must respect her husband. Ephesians 5:33

Christian wedding wishes for a friend

Sending your friends or close acquaintances who are tying the knot Christian wedding wishes can be tough. You are not sure if you should go with something short and sincere or something scripture based. Below are some wedding wishes congratulations for a newly wedded couple:

  • When you say, “I do,” your love for each other becomes a seal upon your hearts, and a seal upon your arms, for love is strong as death, passion as fierce as the grave. Many waters cannot quench love; neither can floods drown it. Grow in love. Today. Tomorrow. Forever.
  • You hold such a cherished place in my heart, and I am grateful to the Lord that you have found someone to love. I pray that the Lord’s favor will be upon your marriage and that he will give you both the wisdom and discernment to guard your beautiful gift of love. God bless you this day and the rest of your life.
  • I am sorry I am unable to be there for your special day. As you celebrate with those who love and cherish you, remember to praise the Lord for his faithfulness. I am praying that the Lord will give you a life full of his love and grace.
  • Cry together, laugh often, pray unceasingly, and always be thankful. May you grow in love together all the days of your lives.
  • Marriage is a lifelong journey. I pray that your love will overflow more and more and that you will keep on growing in knowledge and understanding so that you faithfully accompany one another in this amazing journey together. Congratulations, newlyweds!
  • What a blessing it is to be here today to witness your wedding ceremony. So many people in our world today do not cherish the institution of marriage. I pray that God will bless your union in abundance because you stood up before him and entrusted your relationship into his hands. Congratulations, and God bless you!
  • What a joyous occasion it is for me to see the two of you come together in matrimony. My heart rejoices in knowing that the two of you will be taking on life together. What a lovely couple you are! May the Lord richly bless your marriage!
  • God has brought you together in love. From this moment on, may he guide you in his love through a lifetime of blessings, happiness, and joy.
  • Being married means giving yourself to your spouse every day. Even when you don’t feel like you have much left to give or even when it hurts. However, with all patience, humility, and gentleness, bear with one another in love. God is always with you.
  • Much love to you both on your blessed day! May it be memorable and full of joy as you consecrate your love before the Lord. As you start your new life as man and wife, my prayer is that the Lord will keep you set apart for his glory. May his blessing be upon your marriage always.

Non-cheesy wedding card messages

Sometimes you want your friends to feel how serious and happy you are about their wedding, but you don't know how to say it. You can congratulate a Christian wedding and ask God to bless a marriage using non-cheesy wedding card messages. Here are some non-cheesy Christian wedding quotes for a bride and groom:

  • Wishing you joy, love, and happiness on your wedding day and as you begin your new life together.
  • Congratulations on your marriage, and best wishes for a happy and fulfilling future.
  • I pray God will ensure the love you feel today will always remain in your hearts and never tarnish.
  • By the grace of God, may you both always flourish with every endeavor you undertake.
  • Marriage is a sacred act and a union brought together by God. May His love keep you both together forever.
  • I ask that God watch over and bless you both as you join together as man and wife today.
  • May the Lord keep you both safe and sound, and may your love and wisdom grow with each passing year.
  • May the Lord bless your love, grow it and multiply it, fill your days with happiness, and go through life with you in His perfect love. May He give you all that you desire and more.
  • May your marriage be full of peace and joy, and may you love each other with all your hearts. May you be patient and kind with one another, always speaking kindly to one another.
