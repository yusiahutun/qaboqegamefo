Trampoline park responds to incident

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Tonight, we’re hearing from the Davenport trampoline park, after a fight broke out there Saturday night.

We’re told a fight between juveniles at Altitude Trampoline Park on Elmore Road spilled over to the Super Target parking lot across the street.

Local 4 News was first on the scene.

Late tonight, a manager at Altitude tells us, they did the best they could to handle the many fights and thefts that took place on Saturday night.

They will be hiring more security and staff for weekends, and putting new rules in place to make Altitude a fun and safe place.

They also are asking parents to not leave their kids unsupervised.

Authorities are reviewing surveillance video.

Stay with Local 4 News as the investigation continues.

ORIGINAL STORY:

Local 4 News has learned of a large disturbance Saturday night involving multiple juveniles.

It happened at Altitude Trampoline Park on Elmore Road in Davenport around 10 p.m.

The fight spilled over into the Super Target parking lot across the street.

Local 4 was the first on the scene.

We saw at least five police cars on the scene, along with fire and medic.

There was only one minor injury reported. Local 4 News confirmed a person’s finger was cut.

An investigation is underway. Officials are reviewing surveillance video.

We will bring you more details as we get them.
