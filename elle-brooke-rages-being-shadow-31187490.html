Now it seems 'shadow banning' on social media could be affecting the star's reach on X. It is a technique social media platforms use to restrict the reach of your account by 'blocking' or 'muting' your posts from the platform without being notified.

It usually happens unknowingly, with lots of followers just unable to see the accounts they follow on their main feed. However, it doesn't mean an account has been banned altogether.
