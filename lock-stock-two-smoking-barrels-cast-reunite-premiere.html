Lock, Stock And Two Smoking Barrels cast look completely different as they reunite 25 years after hi

Publish date: 2024-05-13

THE cast of Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels looked completely different as they reunited 25 years after the hit film.

Jason Statham, Jason Flemyng and Dexter Fletcher, who starred as Bacon, Tom and Soap respectively in the film, attended the premiere of Dexter’s new flick Ghosted.

Famous faces gathered to celebrate the launch of the new Apple TV adventure film starring Ana de Armas and Chris Evans.

The A-list event took place at the Ham Yard Hotel in London, and saw the icons gather together for a photo.

Jason, 55, kept it casual in a zip up grey jacket and black trousers, and Jason Flemyng, 56, opted for a beige ensemble.

Dexter, 57, could be seen suited and booted as he stood to the right of his movie co-stars.

The film was released in 1998 and it was made for less than £1million, but took over £20million at the box office.

Discussing the success of the Guy Ritchie movie, Peter McNicholl, who played Vinnie Jones’ cheeky son in the film, said: “Guy knew how big this film would be. At the audition he said, ‘This is going to be massive.’

“Even though it was low budget, he treated it like a blockbuster.”

The film follows the story of a man and his friends who lose a poker game to a gangster and create a plan to pay him back.

The Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels stars were joined by a string of reality stars at the red carpet event.

While some of the movie's stars missed out on the event, having celebrated in New York earlier this week, there were plenty of other celebs gracing the red carpet.

Among them were a number of Love Island stars, including show favourites Jessie Wynter and her boyfriend Will Young.

The farmer was seen in a white shirt and black trousers, while Jessie, 25, looked glam in a black two-piece set.

The couple posed for photos separately from other members of the Love Island cast, including Coco Lodge, who rose to fame last year.

Elsewhere, EastEnders legend Tamzin Outhwaite - who rose to fame as Mel Owen on the show - was all smiles.

She posed alongside comedian Sanjeev Bhaskar and Mad Dogs actor Max Beesley in a green, yellow and brown printed dress.

Looking youthful, Tamzin, 52, let her blonde hair flow down her shoulders as she wore minimal make-up to the event.

The Only Way Is Essex cast also headed to the premiere, including Saffron Lempriere and Chloe Ross.
