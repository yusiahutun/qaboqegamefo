STYLE Edit: Lady Gaga and Angelina Jolie love the Celine 16 bag now Hedi Slimane has designed a

Publish date: 2024-08-21

There is nothing in the world as elegant as a Parisian fashionista. Effortless and chic, these cinematic souls carry themselves in a way that makes it seem that clothes and accessories were made specifically for them.

What makes these intuitive apparel choices seem so special? Is it the cut and fit of the clothing? Is it in the way the outfits are put together, or the colour palettes that are chosen?

Will small handbags become another victim of coronavirus?

It has been argued through the years that one of the things that makes Paris fashion so celebrated is the intentionality of the fashion pieces that are worn. Each distinct item is to be admired, chosen and worn so as to accentuate its companions, and also to stand alone as an iconic piece in its own right.

Take the simple handbag, for example. More than just a functional carry-all, it has developed a cultlike following with millions of disciples around the world. It holds its own in any ensemble, at times being more distinctive than the clothes behind it. Even the way that it’s carried changes the line of an outfit.

Enter the Celine 16. Brainchild of Celine creative director Hedi Slimane, the 16 was designed on his very first day at the brand, in early 2018. Born in the City of Lights, Hedi based his design on the very distinct, Parisian way of wearing a bag. The name itself refers to the Hôtel Colbert at 16, Rue Vivienne, located in the heart of the 2nd arrondissement – the address of Celine’s Paris headquarters.

A celebrity favourite, the bag has been seen on the arms of stars in every major city since its launch, with Reese Witherspoon, Angelina Jolie, Kaia Gerber and Lady Gaga among just the more recent members of the fan club. In a throwback to some of the brand’s previous classic models, Celine has recalled details such as the cropped flap, while the deep drop of the handle allows you to nestle the purse elegantly in the crook of your arm.

Exotic leathers add to the luxury feel. Smooth and sleek, the grained or satinated calfskin leather lends itself to the shine and softness of the premium finish. The colours run the gamut of neutrals to deep jewel tones: a bag for every season. Not to tie themselves to just one aesthetic, the maison has also included a shoulder strap in this collection – a separate, removable wool strap with a smooth leather trim and two “Celine” engraved hooks that can be worn on your shoulder or across your body. Available in two lengths – short, with a 45cm drop and long, with a 52cm drop – to accompany small, medium or large bags in that variety of colours: it adds up to a near-endless number of looks in the collection.

The bag is the kind of piece that evolves with changing trends, balancing its silhouette against the look of the era. Whether with a trench or a summer dress, high-end streetwear or well-fitted denim, the 16 is always comfortable, confident in its carefully stitched edging, plush lambskin lining, and gold tone hardware. The latest Celine 16 will be available from November 2020.
