Zac Efron is showing off his washboard body as he adorably takes his little sister for a day out to the beach on the ‘first day of spring’.

Zac Efron shows he would be a ‘great dad’ and he looks amazing doing it

A third exclaimed: “I CAN TELL HES GOING TO BE A GOOD DAD xxx”

Although it may not be right now, Efron recently said “I think I’d love to have kids one day,” in a conversation with E! News just before his ex Vanessa Hudgens announced her pregnancy.

Zac’s ex Vanessa Hudgens recently announced her pregnancy

In the most iconic way, Zac Efron’s ex, Vanessa Hudgens announced her pregnancy by revealing her bump on the Oscars red carpet.
