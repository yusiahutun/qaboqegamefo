Her debut novel, Nineteen Steps, is set in 1942 London and follows 18-year-old Nellie Morris, who looks set for a romance with air raid warden Billy.

A handsome American soldier soon throws a spanner in the works – but can their relationship survive the harsh realities of World War II?

The book features real-life events taken from Millie’s family history, as her grandmother Ruth miraculously survived the Bethnal Green Tube Disaster back in 1943.

The incident caused the greatest single loss of life in the London tube system, with 173 people – including 62 children – killed when a crush ensued in the staircase to the air raid station.
