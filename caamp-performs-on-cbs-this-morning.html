Caamp Performs On CBS This Morning

Publish date: 2024-02-13

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — On Saturday, Ohio-based folk-rockers Caamp appeared on CBS This Morning Saturday, performing a handful of their hits and fan favorites ahead of their upcoming North American tour.

For the appearance, Caamp performed By and By” as well as a performance of “So Cool” from their 2018 album Boys, and “Mockingbird Song” from their 2016 self-titled album.

Caamp is riding high on a successful 2019, having recently scored their first 1 at radio with their single “ Peach Fuzz” and made their late-night television debut on Jimmy Kimmel Live!

As well, their second studio album “By and By”, was released in 2019 under Mom + Pop Music and landed on the Billboard  200, was the 1 Heatseeker, 1 on the New Artist Alternative Albums, top 5 vinyl album sales for the week and entered the Alternative and Emerging Artist charts.

To date, the band’s music has racked up an impressive 524 million combined streams to date.

Caamp is in the midst of wrapping up their final shows of 2019 with a trio of sold-out performances at Newport Music Hall in Columbus from Dec. 27-Dec. 31st and their 2020 tour kicks off the following month with a sold-out gig at Philadelphia’s Union Transfer.

The tour includes dates with The Lumineers and stops in Madison, Portland, Pittsburgh, Louisville, Charleston and more.
