American actress Dawn-Lyen Gardner is famous for her portrayal of Charley Bordelon-West in the Oprah Winfrey Network's series Queen Sugar.

The actress, who is usually silent about her personal life, was once a married woman and had undergone a terrible divorce in the past. But, her marriage may not be where everyone expected, and the same applies in the case of her divorce.

So let's learn about the unusual married life of Dawn and the cause of her separation!

Onscreen Married Life With Husband, Split After Spoused Gets Mixed In A Scandal

Dawn, who belongs to African-American and Asian ethnicity, is a married woman in the show Queen Sugar.

Dawn aka Charley was married to NBA player, Davis West (played by Timon Kyle Durrett) and has a son with her partner. Charley, an MBA, lived with her husband in Los Angeles and also managed Davis' business affairs.

Charley then confronted Davis in the middle of his basketball game saying,

In the midst of all the scandal, her father suffered a sudden stroke and was rushed to the hospital where he passed away. Dawn divorced her husband and moved back to her hometown in St. Josephine, Louisiana along with her teenage son, Micah. 

Back in her native city, Charley grieved the untimely demise of her father and reconnected with her siblings Nova and Ralph Angel (played by  Rutina Wesley and Kofi Siriboe). Further, she was forced to face her distant mother, Lorna (Sharon Lawrence), who turned up invited.
