Phil McIntyre Out As President Of Roc Nation Management

Publish date: 2024-10-02

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Phil McIntyre has stepped down from his position at Roc Nation Management after less than a year in the role, according to a report in Billboard.

McIntyre whose clients include Rihanna, Shakira, DJ Khaled, and Miguel, among others, was announced as the president of the company’s management division in April.

He first joined Roc Nation in 2015 when his management company, PhilyMack, formed a management partnership with the Jay Z-led company.

According to Variety, McIntyre, and PhilyMack remain affiliated with Roc Nation, but McIntyre plans to step back from his leadership role to focus on his management roster.

Sources also told Variety that McIntyre’s clients Demi Lovato will remain at Roc Nation, where her career will be managed by Lauren Einbender, who was formerly an executive at Philymac.
