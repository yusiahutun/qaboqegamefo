Miley Cyrus' big brother Trace shows off chiseled chest eight months after unhealthy weight gain

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Miley Cyrus' older half-brother Trace showed off his chiseled chest on Wednesday after revealing how much weight he's lost in the span of eight months.

'The picture on the left is what I looked like at the end of last year. I was mentally destroyed and it really affected my physical health,' the Kentucky-born 33-year-old - who boasts 1.3M social media followers - tweeted.

'The pic on the right is me now. Your mindset is everything. It can be your greatest asset or your worst enemy. F*** motivation. Stay dedicated!'

Trace achieved his weight-loss goal with intermittent fasting and doing two workouts a day, six days a week 'no matter what.'

'I feel amazing! I truly don't understand how so many people don't make their health a priority,' Cyrus wrote on June 23.
