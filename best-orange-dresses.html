16 best orange dresses 2023: Bright orange mini dresses to burnt orange midi dresses &amp; more

Publish date: 2024-04-29

One of the hottest colours of 2023 so far, orange is definitely having a moment. Not only is it a welcome pop of colour for any summer outfit, the vibrant shade transitions perfectly through the seasons - so it's more of a staple than you may think. 

From slinky orange midi dresses to bright mini dresses for your upcoming holiday, we’ve searched high and low to find the best orange dresses to shop now that are guaranteed to level up your summer wardrobe.
