About

Jessica Steen is a 58 year old Canadian Actress. Born on 19th December, 1965 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, she is famous for her roles as Linda Metcalf in the ABC drama series Homefront, Dr. Julia Heller in the NBC science fiction series Earth 2, Special Agent Paula Cassidy in the CBS military police procedural series NCIS, Elizabeth "Lisa" Stillman Bartlett in the long-running CBC family drama series Heartland, Constable Donna Sabine in the CTV/CBS/ION Television police procedural crime drama series Flashpoint, and Commissioner Eva Braden in the IFC dark comedy miniseries Bullet in the Face.
