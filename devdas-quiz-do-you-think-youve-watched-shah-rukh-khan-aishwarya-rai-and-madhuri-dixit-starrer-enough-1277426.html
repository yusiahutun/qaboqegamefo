Publish date: 2024-06-05

Sanjay Leela Bhansali is a celebrated, multi-talented Bollywood filmmaker, famous for his larger-than-life projects. Among his many significant works, Devdas stands out as a timeless cult-classic film. Shah Rukh Khan played the lead role, accompanied by Aishwarya Rai, Madhuri Dixit, Jackie Shroff, and others. This incredible adaptation of the book to the big screen tells the tragic love story of Devdas, a wealthy law graduate who returns from London to marry his childhood friend, Parvati, also known as Paro. However, fate had different plans, leading to his ultimate misery. Even years after its release, this romantic drama continues to capture the hearts of audiences.
