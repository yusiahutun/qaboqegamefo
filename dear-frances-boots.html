Dear Frances Shoes and Boots

Publish date: 2024-09-12

With fall sneakily (and quickly) approaching, it's time to start putting boots on the brain. Luckily, your favorite A-list style stars have already done the leg work and discovered this season's must-have bootie. Enter Dear Frances, a buzzy new shoe brand that's already become a hot favorite amongst celebrities like Selena Gomez, Bella Hadid, Emma Roberts, Kylie and Kendall Jenner, and its sleek 'Spirit' boot.

[instagram ]https://www.instagram.com/p/BI5sk6oDiwk/?taken-by=dear_frances&hl=en[/instagram]

The mid-calf boot has cemented its MVP-status amongst street style stars and young It-girls alike—punctuating many a model off-duty outfit along the way. And for those of us (aka all of us) in search of the perfect bootie for fall: this might just be it. With a sturdy block heel, gold zipper and four different styles to choose from (black leather, camel croc, blue velvet and maroon leather), the Spirit boot is versatile enough to pair with any fall wardrobe—even if you're Emma Roberts leaving the Met Gala.

Getty Images

Dear Frances, which just made its US launch in Spring 2016 and debuted its new fall collection earlier this week, is surprisingly accessible with prices ranging from $340- $660. With sleek designs, intricate details and and a cult following of A-listers, this is one shoe brand to put on your radar.

You can shop the Dear Frances Spirit Boots for $550 at dearfrances.com.
