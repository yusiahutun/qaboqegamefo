Golf Galaxy Performance Center Hosts Grand Opening

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Dick’s Sporting Goods will host a grand opening celebration for its new Golf Galaxy Performance Center store in Port St. Lucie, from September 29 through October 1. The Golf Galaxy Performance Center features state-of-the-art hitting bays with TrackMan technology, a putting green, golf services desk, instruction from a PGA Professional, and must-have gear.

To mark the occasion, guests can stop by all weekend to win prizes and giveaways hosted by brands like PING, Callaway, TaylorMade, Nike, TravisMathew, G/FORE, CALIA Golf, and more.

Additionally, Good Good, the talented team of golf content creators who recently started their own line of golf clothing, Good Good Golf, will host a meet-and-greet, on September 29, from 5 to 7 p.m.

Each day of the grand opening celebration, the first 100 guests aged 18 and older in line will receive a  free Golf Galaxy mystery gift card, with one lucky person winning a $500 gift card. Additionally, all customers can compete in a Closest to the Pin Contest and receive 50 percent off all golf services, with ScoreCard Members earning three times the points on any purchase. Customers can also shop a variety of grand opening deals throughout the weekend.

The new Golf Galaxy Performance Center is located at The Landing at Tradition (10512 SW Village Parkway) in Port St. Lucie. For more information, visit golfgalaxy.com.
