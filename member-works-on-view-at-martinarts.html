Member Works on View at MartinArts

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Escape the summer heat and spend afternoons touring MartinArts‘ latest exhibition, “Infinite Possibilities: A Members Showcase,” opening to the public June 13. Works by member artists will take over the organization’s Court House Cultural Center gallery space in Stuart and will be on view through July 28.

 

“Infinite Possibilities” will showcase the diverse talents of local artists—who are all members of MartinArts—with a unique twist. Each piece in the exhibit was created and will be presented in a rectangular shape. A selection of original works created in a variety of mediums will be available for purchase. All proceeds from the sale of art will benefit arts education programs across Martin County.

MartinArts will welcome art patrons to an opening reception June 16, from 5:30 to 7 p.m.
