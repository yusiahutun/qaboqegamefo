Bloodlands star James Nesbitt defends using his own accent in shows

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Have you been enjoying Bloodlands? 

    Bloodlands star James Nesbitt has opened up about receiving criticism for using his Northern Irish accent in his TV shows, admitting that some people say he always does the same thing. 

    MORE: James Nesbitt talks how Bloodlands helped him spend time with his father before his death

    Chatting to he said: “A lot of the naysayers go – because I always do my own accent – ‘Oh, he always does the same bloody thing.’ But I would say that Tom Hanks is always just using his own accent too.” 

    WATCH: Have you been watching Bloodlands?

    He continued: “The idea of having a leading character in a primetime comedy drama speaking with my accent but having no real political baggage was deemed quite unusual. I’m lucky to have been involved in getting that accent into living rooms all over Britain, where I could put a dent in the notion that that sound was only associated with conflict.”

    James is currently starring in Bloodlands

    Joking about his role in the superhero show he added: “There’s only ever going to be one middle-aged, Northern Irish superhero created by Stan Lee, and it’s me.”
