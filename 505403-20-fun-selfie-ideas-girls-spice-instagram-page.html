20 fun selfie ideas for girls to spice up your Instagram page

Publish date: 2024-02-09

Despite the popularity of selfies, it can be challenging to get an incredible picture– especially if you want to show off your best side. But amazingly, there are many selfie ideas for girls to keep your social media exciting and fresh. You’ll love how simple and fun they are.

The poses you try when taking a selfie are crucial in determining whether it'll be a hit or a miss. You might have the best lighting, makeup, and clothing, but the selfie won't come out great if you pose poorly.

Selfie ideas for girls

Today, selfies are so popular because of smartphones with powerful front cameras. Here are some fun, cute selfie poses for girls to make you stand out.

1. Tilt your head upwards

Girls love taking selfies while holding their phones in front of their faces. But with this selfie idea, you'll have to keep your phone slightly higher than your head level. It forces you to tilt your head upwards. Doing so will bring out your face's structure better.

2. Put on an easy-going smile

Taking selfies alone feels awkward, but a smile will make the photo appear better. If you find it hard to smile alone, you can perfect it in front of the mirror before taking the selfie. When taking a selfie, try hard to give a more natural smile to win hearts.

3. Take a mirror selfie

Mirror selfies are beautiful and easy to take, and that's why they're famous. The trick about mirror selfies is creativity; there are many ways to be creative. You can use a car's mirror, shop windows, or bathroom mirrors. All you need to do is ensure the reflections are clear.

4. Find unique locations

Selfie poses ideas are much better when the background is something exciting. You can strike a selfie in nature, a famous landmark, or a well-known city. Make sure you use natural lighting as much as you can. The best time to take this selfie is during blue hour or golden hour. Avoid the harsh midday sun.

5. Take a selfie while doing exciting activities or use unusual poses

Most girls take selfies while sitting or standing. But you can spice up things by capturing yourself in more exciting positions. For example, you can capture yourself while doing yoga, gymnastics, or more. There are many ways to be creative when taking selfies; ensure they're safe.

6. Go underwater

Are you a good swimmer? If yes, you can take underwater selfies as they are fun and unique. You can use an underwater camera or a waterproof smartphone. Make the selfie livelier by keeping your eyes open or smiling.

7. Capture a selfie while on your hobby

If you love riding bikes, you can take a selfie while on your bicycle. If you're a great cook or like baking, you can take a selfie while preparing your best meal. The best funny selfie ideas are those captured when one is having a great time.

8. Snap a pic with your pet

Are you looking for hot selfie poses ideas? If so, then you should try this one. Pets are fun and can be great posing partners. However, you'll need patients when dealing with pets because they don't like staying in one position.

You should also ensure that the pet is comfortable when posing. There are several ways to take selfies with your pet. You can do it while hugging them or gently holding them.

9. Capture moments with friends

You don't have to be alone in selfies to look good; you can take a group selfie that involves your friends. The person taking the selfie should stand further from others to fully capture everyone. Alternatively, the photographer can stand at a lower or higher spot to capture everyone.

This idea also works well with mirror selfies. Luckily, many places, such as bars and art galleries, have attractive mirrors.

10. Takes selfies from unusual angles

Most girls take selfies from an angle just above their eye level. But you can be different by taking it from unusual angles. For example, you can capture a selfie from the ground; it offers an exciting perspective to your picture. Or use a selfie stick as it provides many new angles to experiment with and will help you to avoid facial distortions.

11. Include your hand for a natural-looking portrait

Including your hand in your selfie pose will make it look more natural. You can gently touch your palm, chin, or face while taking the pic. This is among the easy selfie poses for girls. That’s why it’s common to see selfies of girls while they're playing with or lifting their beautiful hair. It makes them look more candid.

12. Make your eyes stand out

Eyes are beautiful and always the focus when it comes to selfies; ensure they stand out. You can do this by looking straight into the camera. If you have brightly-colored eyes and want to open them more, use a selfie stick and raise it in the air. Ensure the lighting is excellent; it’ll make the selfie more precise and attractive. This idea is incredible for a single photo pose for girl selfie.

13. Relax your lips

Have you ever wondered why professional models have soft and attractive lips? It's because they've mastered how to relax them. To achieve this pose, slightly open your mouth and breathe through it. It'll make your lips look fuller. Note that it's one of the cute selfie poses for Snapchat.

14. Take selfies using accessories and props

Accessories and props are great additions that’ll make your selfies stand out. Your props can be classy, funny, or fashionable. Put on a face that complements the prop.

15. Lay down and pose

Spice up your selfie game by simply laying down on the grass, floor, or bed and taking a pic from a higher angle. You can take things to another level by posing with a friend while lying down with heads in opposite directions.

16. Take a selfie near a window

If you love indoor selfies, this idea is for you. The light from the window will bring out your face clearly and beautifully. You'll surely love how gorgeous the selfie will look.

17. Include colorful lighting

This idea is best for girls looking to pose in dimly lit places. Look for areas with colorful billboards, lights, or neon signs, and take a selfie. The colors will enhance the personality of your pic.

18. Show your best silly poses

You can try expressions that make you laugh. These silly poses give an easy and playful vibe but often seem unnatural. Although this shouldn't worry you, have fun and try as many poses as possible.

19. Two-handed selfie

Girls commonly take selfies using a single hand. You can change things by using both hands. Using both hands stabilizes the camera, and they'll work as frames for the picture.

20. Add depth with a foreground element

You can make your selfie extra immersive by adding some depth. Do this by simply placing something between the camera and your face. For example, you can put a bunch of leaves, branches, or flowers. But ensure the focus is on your face and that it's not obstructed.
