I Feel Sexier Than Ever in This Gooseberry Intimates Swimsuit

Publish date: 2024-06-30

For as long as I can remember, I have been on the hunt for the perfect one-piece swimsuit. Here is my checklist: It has to be flattering and offer some sort of chest support for my 34Ds, have a high-ish (but not too high) cut in the front and the back, plus thin straps that are adjustable. I can officially announce that my search is over thanks to the Gooseberry Intimates So Chic One-Piece Tank Swimsuit. 

The Australian brand popped up on my radar after I saw a slew of celebrities wearing the plunging version of the swimmie, including Blake Lively (who just wore it on vacation!), Jennifer Lopez, Sydney Sweeney, Kendall Jenner, and Kaia Gerber. While that style is undeniably sexy, I gravitated towards the square neckline because it was a little more modest; after all, I am a mother-of-two who spends a good amount of the summer in places that give citations for too-sexy swimsuits. The swimsuit is available in six colors, including black, blue, and rust orange and sizes XS to XL. 

I can personally attest that even the One-Piece Tank Swimsuit turns up the heat level just enough — and then some. I was warned that the suit runs a little small as its intention is to shape and sculpt, however, I was still a little startled at just how body-clinging the double-lined bathing suit felt when I first slipped it on. (The brand offers a sizing calculator, though I generally would advise to order one size up.) However, it was super comfy and fit well once it was fully on and I adjusted the straps. The best part? It was super flattering on my waist, chest, and backside; my legs even felt longer than ever thanks to the high cut. Bottom line: I’ve never felt so sexy in a one-piece. 

I debuted the  Gooseberry Intimates swimsuit for the first time during a trip to Hawaii last summer. Not only does it look awesome alone, but it doubles as a bodysuit with a skirt, cutoffs, or pants — you could seriously wear it for a night out. After sharing some photos on social media, my inbox flooded with inquiries about the suit. People were shocked to learn that the pricey-looking one-piece was just $99, significantly less than most celebrity-endorsed swimsuits. 

My advice? Shop the Gooseberry Intimates So Chic One-Piece Tank Swimsuit before summer arrives — it may just sell out.
