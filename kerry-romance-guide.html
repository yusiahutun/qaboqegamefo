Kerry Romance Guide - Cyberpunk 2077 Guide

Publish date: 2024-09-20

Depending on which characters you meet and how you interact with them in Cyberpunk 2077 you may be able to find love in Night City. In this section of our Romance Guide we will explain where to meet and how to romance Kerry.

When You'll Meet KerryKerry is introduced in an Act 2 Side Job called Holdin' On.

How to Romance Kerry

Listed below is every Side Job and dialogue option that you will need to select in order to romance Kerry.

Side Job: Chippin' In

To kick off this romance questline, you’ll need to first help Rogue get information on Adam Smasher in the mission Chippin' In.

  • At the conclusion of the mission, make sure you stick around and chat with Rogue
  • When you head out to the oil fields with Johnny, if you play nice with him, you’ll get a sweet mission that involves a date with Rogue. If not, you’ll skip ahead right to the mission involving Kerry.
  • The date with Rogue isn’t required to start the romance quest line with Kerry, but it is required for one of the many endings in Cyberpunk 2077, so it’s probably worth being nice to Johnny.

Side Job: Holdin' On

advertisement

You’ll first meet Kerry during the mission Holdin’ On.

  • Sneak into his house, let Johnny take over, and let the two former band mates reunite and catch each other up on their lives.
  • Eventually Kerry will float the idea to get their band, Samurai, back together, which will send you on your next quest “Second Conflict”

Side Job: Second Conflict

There are a number of ways the Second Conflict quest can go, largely depending on your choices and actions in the quest The Pickup way back in Act 1, but ultimately the result will be you rescuing Nancy and convincing her to reunite with Samurai for one last gig.

  • If you’ve rescued Brick in “The Pickup,” this will be as easy as being invited through the front door, talking to Brick, and then walking out with Nancy
  • If you haven’t rescued Brick, you’re going to have to fight your way through and actually rescue Nancy.

Side Job: A Like Supreme

In the next quest A Like Supreme, you’ll have to decide whether you want to play with either Denny or Henry, but ultimately the choice doesn’t matter for the purpose of romancing Kerry.

  • After you finish rocking the house and complete “A Like Supreme,” you’ll need to pass some time doing some other quests and waiting at least 24 hours.
  • Eventually, Kerry should call you and start the next quest Rebel Rebel.

Side Job: Rebel Rebel

In the quest Rebel Rebel, you’ll need to stop a truck from delivering instruments to a J-Pop band that’s performing a cover of one of Kerry’s songs.

There aren’t any major choices to be made here, but if your cool attribute is high, the drivers might be able to escape with their lives… If you let them.

Side Job: I Don't Wanna Hear It

At the conclusion of Rebel Rebel, do some more waiting (or skipping time 24 hours at a time) and answering text messages from Kerry, until eventually you get a call that will kick off the next quest, I Don't Wanna Hear It.

In this quest you’ll need to infiltrate a concert, and there are a ton of ways to do it.

  • In the alley to the left of the venue you can jump up to the fire escape if you have a double jump or a high jump

Also in the alley you can scan the faces of the two fans with VIP tickets to digitally steal their tickets

advertisement

  • In the back of the line you can buy tickets from a scalper for 21,000 eddies
  • To the right of the venue there’s a shutter door that you can open with enough strength, along with a door you can open by hacking the digital lock next to it if you have enough intelligence. Doing this will skip the next step since you’ll be backstage already.

Once inside, you’ll need to sneak backstage. You can do this easily if you have enough points in your “cool” stat to smooth talk the guard into letting you pass. Otherwise, you’ll have to distract the roadie at the bar and let Kerry steal his credentials.

Once backstage, simply talk with Kerry by the door and you’ll trigger a conversation with Us Cracks. How you approach this is up to you. It’ll affect the next mission, but not your relationship with Kerry.

Side Job: Off The Leash

The next mission, Off The Leash, is the make it or break it point in Kerry’s relationship questline. When he starts opening up to you, make sure to tell him "You can tell me" and then when given the opportunity, kiss him to seal the deal.

Side Job: Boat Drinks

Pass some time again and you’ll get the next mission, Boat Drinks, which is where you’ll get the option to get hot and steamy with Kerry.

  • All you have to do is kiss him when given the opportunity.
  • When you wash up on the beach, you’re given one more opportunity to cement the relationship by giving Kerry a hug. Do it, and you’ve completed Kerry’s romance quest line!
