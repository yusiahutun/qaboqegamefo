Kristen Stewart wears a Chanel logo onesie to sit front row at Karl Lagerfeld's final Chanel show

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Kristen Stewart was one of the front row guests who arrived in Paris to see Karl Lagerfeld's final collection for Chanel today (5 March). The actress, who has long been an ambassador for the French fashion house and worked closely with the late designer, arrived at the autumn/winter 2019 show at the Grand Palais wearing a warming onesie.

The quilted zip-front jumpsuit featured a repeating pattern of Chanel's iconic interlocking c logo. She accessorised the outfit with heeled Mary-Jane shoes and red-framed sunglasses.

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

She finished the look with layered necklaces, a burgundy smoky eye and her blonde-tipped hair in a tousled style.

Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

The emotional show, which featured some of Lagerfeld's last ever designs, saw models crying on the catwalk. The audience also observed a one-minute silence at the start of the show as a mark of respect.

IAN LANGSDON/EPA-EFE/REX/Shutterstock

Cara Delevingne lead the powerful finale. The show also saw Penelope Cruz making her catwalk debut.

Largerfeld passed away in Paris on 19 February at the age of 85. He held the title of creative director at Chanel - as well as at Fendi and his own eponymous label - up until his death.
