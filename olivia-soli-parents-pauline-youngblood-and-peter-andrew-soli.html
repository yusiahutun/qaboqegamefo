Pauline Youngblood And Peter Andrew Soli

Publish date: 2024-03-10

It was quite a back story Olivia Soli gave away during her American Idol audition. As she auditioned in Las Vegas singing “Hello” by Lionel Richie she tearfully talked about how close she is with her sister, and how feels like she is living out the Idol dream for both of them. But, what about her parents? Is Season 21’s most notable vocal standout also as much fond of her parents? Let us tell you in the rest of the writing.

American Idol: Who Are Olivia Soli’s Parents?

Olivia Soli is a Los Angeles native and singer-songwriter who recently performed the national anthem at a Los Angeles Clippers game, before coming to Idol. She was born to her parents 20 years prior from now, in 2002.

Oliva did not talk much about the other members of her family, other than her sister. Nonetheless, she has to have a good relationship with all of them, especially her parents.

Keep reading as we tell you more about her parents, individually and together.

Meet Pauline Youngblood, Olivia Soli’s Mother

Olivia Soli’s mother is Pauline Youngblood, daughter of Beatrice Dixon.

In October 2008, Pauline, unfortunately, lost her brother Robert Lee Youngblood. Per his obituary from that time, he left behind fond loving memories to his wife of many years, Tamara Youngblood, sons, Michael (Rasmiyyah) Youngblood and Jason Youngblood, and grandchildren, Kaylah Youngblood, Myleah Youngblood, and Rikayah Youngblood.

Other than Pauline, Robert is survived by sisters, Hilda Walker, Onnie Roberson, Lucille Griffin, Sabrina Jones, Johnnie Mae Deloach, and brothers, Craig Dixon, Mark Dixon, and Jeremy Dixon.

  • Pauline Youngblood Age

Pauline H Soli was born in June 1966. So, he turned 56 years old in 2022.

  • Pauline Youngblood Job

On her IG, Pauline Youngblood goes around as the founder of Youngblood Cosmetics. On her LinkedIn as well, she mentions being the President and CEO of a mineral cosmetic company in Simi Valley, California from September 1996 until now.

  • Is Pauline Youngblood On Instagram?

Yes. Pauline Youngblood can be found on Instagram. As of 24 April 2023, his IG @pauline_youngblood included 13 posts and 775 followers.

Meet Peter Andrew Soli, Olivia Soli Father

Like Pauline, Peter Andrew also lost his brother. He was Giancarlo Soli and he not only was his best friend but also his daughter Olivia’s “biggest fan and a role model”. The late Giancarlo was an MD at Signature Care Emergency Center and founder and CEO at Hooley. Before that, he studied Emergency medicine at UConn.

Giancarlo is also survived by his other sibling, his sister Laura Soli Capitani, who turned 67 years old in May 2022. She also is the mother to grown-up kids: Simona Capitani and Giulia Capitani.

  • Peter Andrew Soli Age

Peter Andrew Soli was born on 28 May 1950. So, he turned 72 years old in 2022.

  • Peter Andrew Soli Job

As per the internet, Peter Andrew Soli has had a career as a personal injury lawyer in Los Angeles, California. And on LinkedIn, he also mentioned being the CEO at Youngblood Mineral Cosmetics. This should be an affair from the past though because the very company is now handled/owned by none other than Pauline.

While on Facebook, he revealed that he studied Drama and Theater Arts/Political Science at UC Santa Barbara and also went to The American School in Switzerland (TASIS).

In between all this, Peter also managed to fulfill his desire to act. IMDB introduces him as an actor and producer known for The Gallant Men (1962), The Admired of 2016, and 2017.

  • Peter Andrew Soli On Instagram?

Not on Instagram, but one could locate Peter Andrew on Twitter @pandrewsolica and ‘Peter Andrew Soli’ Facebook.
